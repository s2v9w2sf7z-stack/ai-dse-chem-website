DSE 化學筆記：課題四 酸和鹽基

1. 酸與鹼基的四大核心反應

不論是強酸還是弱酸，它們在水溶液中都有相似的化學性質：

  • 酸 + 活潑金屬 $\rightarrow$ 鹽 + 氫氣
    $Mg(s) + 2HCl(aq) \rightarrow MgCl_2(aq) + H_2(g)$
  • 酸 + 碳酸鹽 / 碳酸氫鹽 $\rightarrow$ 鹽 + 水 + 二氧化碳
    $Na_2CO_3(s) + 2HCl(aq) \rightarrow 2NaCl(aq) + H_2O(l) + CO_2(g)$
  • 酸 + 鹽基（中和反應） $\rightarrow$ 鹽 + 水
    $CuO(s) + H_2SO_4(aq) \rightarrow CuSO_4(aq) + H_2O(l)$

2. 核心考點：強酸與弱酸的分辨

定義區分：
  • 強酸（如 $HCl$）：在水中完全電離。 $HCl(aq) \rightarrow H^+(aq) + Cl^-(aq)$
  • 弱酸（如 $CH_3COOH$）：在水中部分電離，化學反應屬可逆反應。 $CH_3COOH(aq) \rightleftharpoons CH_3COO^-(aq) + H^+(aq)$
相同摩爾濃度（如 $0.1\,M$）時的物理性質比較：
性質項目強酸 ($0.1\,M\,HCl$)弱酸 ($0.1\,M\,CH_3COOH$)
氫離子濃度 $[H^+]$較高 ($0.1\,M$)較低（遠小於 $0.1\,M$）
pH 值較低 (pH = 1)較高 (pH $\approx$ 3)
導電性較強（因自由移動離子濃度較高）較弱（因自由移動離子濃度較低）
與鎂反應的速率較快（氣泡產生劇烈）較慢（氣泡產生緩慢）

3. 酸鹼滴定與計算基礎

滴定的核心公式： 摩爾數 ($n$) = 摩爾濃度 ($M$) $\times$ 體積 ($V$, 單位必須為 $dm^3$)

常用指示劑終點轉色（由酸入鹼）：

  • 酚酞 (Phenolphthalein)：無色 (Colorless) $\rightarrow$ 粉紅色 (Pink)
  • 甲基橙 (Methyl orange)：紅色 (Red) $\rightarrow$ 黃色 (Yellow)（終點為橙色）

DSE Chemistry Notes: Topic 4 Acids and Bases

1. Four Core Reactions of Acids

Both strong and weak acids show similar chemical properties in aqueous solutions:

  • Acid + Reactive Metal $\rightarrow$ Salt + Hydrogen
    $Mg(s) + 2HCl(aq) \rightarrow MgCl_2(aq) + H_2(g)$
  • Acid + Carbonate / Hydrogencarbonate $\rightarrow$ Salt + Water + Carbon Dioxide
    $Na_2CO_3(s) + 2HCl(aq) \rightarrow 2NaCl(aq) + H_2O(l) + CO_2(g)$
  • Acid + Base (Neutralization) $\rightarrow$ Salt + Water
    $CuO(s) + H_2SO_4(aq) \rightarrow CuSO_4(aq) + H_2O(l)$

2. Examination Hotspot: Strong vs. Weak Acids

Definitions:
  • Strong Acid (e.g., $HCl$): Completely ionized in water. $HCl(aq) \rightarrow H^+(aq) + Cl^-(aq)$
  • Weak Acid (e.g., $CH_3COOH$): Partially ionized in water; the reaction is reversible. $CH_3COOH(aq) \rightleftharpoons CH_3COO^-(aq) + H^+(aq)$
Comparison at the same molarity (e.g., $0.1\,M$):
PropertyStrong Acid ($0.1\,M\,HCl$)Weak Acid ($0.1\,M\,CH_3COOH$)
Hydrogen ion conc. $[H^+]$Higher ($0.1\,M$)Lower (much less than $0.1\,M$)
pH valueLower (pH = 1)Higher (pH $\approx$ 3)
Electrical ConductivityHigher (higher concentration of mobile ions)Lower (lower concentration of mobile ions)
Reaction rate with MgFaster (vigorous effervescence)Slower (mild effervescence)

3. Acid-Base Titration & Basic Calculations

Core Formula: Number of moles ($n$) = Molarity ($M$) $\times$ Volume ($V$ in $dm^3$)

Common Indicator Color Changes (from acidic to basic medium):

  • Phenolphthalein: Colorless $\rightarrow$ Pink
  • Methyl orange: Red $\rightarrow$ Yellow (Orange at end point)

Bilingual Glossary

English TermChinese TranslationDefinition
Basicity鹽基度The maximum number of $H^+$ ions that can be produced by one molecule of an acid upon complete ionization.

Equivalence point當量點The point at which the amount of acid and base added matches the stoichiometric ratio in the chemical equation.

✍️ 自我檢測練習（課題四：酸和鹽基） ✍️ Self-Assessment Quiz (Topic 4: Acids and Bases)

Q1. 鹽酸 ($HCl(aq)$) 是一種強酸，而乙酸 ($CH_3COOH(aq)$) 是一種弱酸。從微觀電離的角度，解釋「強酸」與「弱酸」的分別。

Q1. Hydrochloric acid ($HCl(aq)$) is a strong acid, whereas ethanoic acid ($CH_3COOH(aq)$) is a weak acid. Explain the difference between a "strong acid" and a "weak acid" in terms of ionization at the microscopic level.

查看答案及詳細解析 View Answer and Detailed Explanation

正確答案：
強酸在水中會完全電離 (Completely ionize)，釋放出所有的氫離子 ($H^+$)；
弱酸在水中只會部分電離 (Partially ionize)，只有極少部分的酸分子會轉化為氫離子。

精修解析： 這是 DSE 極高頻的解釋題！切記「強弱」與「濃稀」是完全不同的概念。強/弱指的是電離的程度，而濃/稀指的是溶質在水中的比例。因此，即便是 0.001 M 的極稀鹽酸，它依然是強酸！

Correct Answer:
A strong acid completely ionizes in water to release all its hydrogen ions ($H^+$).
A weak acid only partially ionizes in water, with only a very small fraction of acid molecules converting into hydrogen ions.

Detailed Explanation: This is a highly frequent explanation question in DSE! Remember that "strong/weak" and "concentrated/dilute" are entirely different concepts. Strong/weak refers to the degree of ionization, while concentrated/dilute refers to the proportion of solute in the solution. Therefore, even a very dilute 0.001 M hydrochloric acid is still a strong acid!

Q2. 將適量的稀氫氯酸 ($HCl(aq)$) 加入含有碳酸鈣固體 ($CaCO_3(s)$) 的試管中。描述兩項可見的實驗現象，並寫出相關的化學方程式（需包含物態符號）。

Q2. Add an appropriate amount of dilute hydrochloric acid ($HCl(aq)$) into a test tube containing solid calcium carbonate ($CaCO_3(s)$). Describe TWO observable experimental phenomena and write the relevant chemical equation (including state symbols).

查看答案及詳細解析 View Answer and Detailed Explanation

正確答案：
實驗現象：1. 白色固體溶解 (或體積變小)；2. 產生無色氣泡 (或劇烈泡騰)。

Correct Answer:
Observations: 1. The white solid dissolves (or decreases in size); 2. Colourless gas bubbles are evolved (or vigorous effervescence).

CaCO₃ (s) + 2HCl (aq) → CaCl₂ (aq) + H₂O (l) + CO₂ (g)

精修解析： 酸與碳酸鹽的反應是必記的四大核心反應之一。在回答「觀察現象」時，不要寫「產生二氧化碳」，因為氣體的化學名稱是無法直接用肉眼「觀察」到的，必須寫「產生無色氣泡」。

Detailed Explanation: The reaction between an acid and a carbonate is one of the four core reactions you must memorize. When answering "observable phenomena", never write "carbon dioxide is produced" because the chemical name of a gas cannot be "observed" directly by naked eyes; you must write "colourless gas bubbles are evolved".

Q3. 學生將 $25.0 \, cm^3$ 未知濃度的氫氧化鈉溶液 ($NaOH(aq)$) 移至錐形瓶，並加入兩滴甲基橙 (Methyl orange) 指示劑。隨後他以標準硫酸溶液 ($H_2SO_4(aq)$) 進行滴定。指出滴定終點 (End point) 時溶液的顏色變化，並指出硫酸的「元數」(Basicity)。

Q3. A student transfers $25.0 \, cm^3$ of sodium hydroxide solution ($NaOH(aq)$) of unknown concentration into a conical flask and adds two drops of methyl orange indicator. He then titrates it with a standard sulphuric acid solution ($H_2SO_4(aq)$). State the colour change of the solution at the end point, and state the "basicity" of sulphuric acid.

查看答案及詳細解析 View Answer and Detailed Explanation

正確答案：
顏色變化：由黃色變為橙色。
硫酸的鹽基度：2（二元酸，Dibasic acid）。

精修解析： 【DSE 死亡陷阱！】錐形瓶內原本是氫氧化鈉（鹼性），甲基橙在鹼性中呈黃色，滴入酸至中和點時會變成橙色（若滴加過量酸則變紅）。很多同學會把顏色倒轉寫成「由紅變黃」，這會被全扣分！另外，硫酸分子 $H_2SO_4$ 在完全電離時能釋放 2 個氫離子，因此元數為 2。

Correct Answer:
Colour change: From yellow to orange.
Basicity of sulphuric acid: 2 (Dibasic acid).

Detailed Explanation: [DSE Deadly Trap!] The conical flask originally contains sodium hydroxide (alkaline). Methyl orange is yellow in alkaline solutions and turns orange at the equivalence point (it turns red if excess acid is added). Many students mistakenly write the color change in reverse as "from red to yellow", which results in zero marks! Additionally, a sulphuric acid molecule ($H_2SO_4$) releases 2 hydrogen ions upon complete ionization, hence its basicity is 2.
