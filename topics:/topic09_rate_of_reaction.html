DSE 化學筆記：課題九 反應速率

1. 反應速率的量度方法

選擇量度方法取決於反應物及生成物的物理狀態：

  • 量度氣體體積 (Volume of gas)：使用氣筒收集。適合產生不溶於水或微溶於水氣體的反應（如 $H_2, CO_2$）。
  • 量度質量減少 (Mass loss)：將燒杯放在電子磅上。適合產生高密度氣體（如 $CO_2$）的反應。不適用於產生 $H_2$ 的反應，因為 $H_2$ 太輕，質量變化不明顯。
  • 量度透光度/沉澱物 (Disappearing cross)：如硫代硫酸鈉與稀酸反應產生黃色硫沉澱 ($S$)。
    $S_2O_3^{2-}(aq) + 2H^+(aq) \rightarrow S(s) + SO_2(g) + H_2O(l)$

2. 碰撞理論 (Collision Theory) 滿分解釋框架

解釋為何反應速率隨時間減慢：
隨着反應進行，反應物的濃度下降 $\rightarrow$ 單位體積內的粒子數目減少 $\rightarrow$ 粒子的碰撞頻率下降 $\rightarrow$ 有效碰撞頻率下降 $\rightarrow$ 速率減慢。

3. 影響反應速率的關鍵因素

因素微觀解釋 (根據碰撞理論)
濃度 (Concentration)單位體積內的反應物粒子數量增加，導致碰撞頻率增加。
固體表面積 (Surface Area)粉末提供更大的表面積，暴露更多粒子，導致碰撞頻率增加。
溫度 (Temperature)粒子平均動能增加。具有大於或等於活化能 ($E_a$) 的粒子數目顯著增加，導致有效碰撞頻率增加。
催化劑 (Catalyst)提供一條活化能 ($E_a$) 較低的另一反應途徑。更多粒子能跨越此能量門檻，導致有效碰撞頻率增加。
考試陷阱 (DSE Trap)：
加入催化劑 不會 改變反應物和生成物的焓 ($\Delta H$)，也 不會 增加生成物的總產量 (Yield)。它只能讓你更快得到相同數量的生成物。

DSE Chemistry Notes: Topic 9 Rate of Reaction

1. Methods of Measuring Reaction Rate

The choice of method depends on the physical states of reactants and products:

  • Volume of gas: Using a gas syringe. Suitable for reactions producing gases (e.g., $H_2, CO_2$).
  • Mass loss: Using an electronic balance. Suitable for reactions producing heavy gases (e.g., $CO_2$). Not suitable for $H_2$ because it is too light to show a significant mass change.
  • Turbidity / Disappearing cross: e.g., reaction between sodium thiosulphate and dilute acid producing yellow sulphur precipitate ($S$).
    $S_2O_3^{2-}(aq) + 2H^+(aq) \rightarrow S(s) + SO_2(g) + H_2O(l)$

2. Collision Theory: Perfect Answering Framework

Explaining why rate decreases over time:
As the reaction proceeds, the concentration of reactants decreases $\rightarrow$ Number of particles per unit volume decreases $\rightarrow$ Collision frequency decreases $\rightarrow$ Effective collision frequency decreases $\rightarrow$ Rate decreases.

3. Key Factors Affecting Rate

FactorMicroscopic Explanation (Collision Theory)
ConcentrationMore reactant particles per unit volume, leading to an increase in collision frequency.
Surface Area (Solid)Powder provides a larger surface area, exposing more particles, leading to an increase in collision frequency.
TemperatureAverage kinetic energy of particles increases. A larger fraction of particles possess energy $\ge$ activation energy ($E_a$), leading to a significant increase in effective collision frequency.
CatalystProvides an alternative reaction pathway with a lower activation energy ($E_a$). More particles can overcome this energy barrier, leading to an increase in effective collision frequency.
DSE Trap:
Adding a catalyst DOES NOT change the enthalpy ($\Delta H$) of reactants/products, and it DOES NOT increase the total yield of products. It only allows you to obtain the same amount of product faster.

Bilingual Glossary

English TermChinese TranslationDefinition

✍️ 自我檢測練習（課題九：反應速率） ✍️ Self-Assessment Quiz (Topic 9: Rate of Reaction)

Q1. 根據碰撞理論（Collision theory），解釋為什麼升高溫度會顯著增加化學反應的速率。

Q1. Based on the collision theory, explain why increasing the temperature significantly increases the rate of a chemical reaction.

查看答案及詳細解析 View Answer and Detailed Explanation

正確答案：
1. 升高溫度時，反應物分子的平均動能增加，分子運動速度加快，使分子間的碰撞頻率（或每秒總碰撞次數）增加。
2. 更重要的是，能量大於或等於活化能（$\ge E_a$）的分子百分比（比例）顯著增加。
3. 因此，每秒發生的有效碰撞次數（Frequency of effective collisions）增加，反應速率隨之上升。

精修解析： 【DSE 滿分黃金框架】這是一道價值 3 分的標準碰撞理論題。考評局的評卷參考非常看重第 2 點：必須提到「分子百分比/比例（fraction/proportion）」，如果只寫「有更多分子擁有大於 $E_a$ 的能量」，有機會被扣掉 1 分！

Correct Answer:
1. When the temperature increases, the average kinetic energy of reactant molecules increases, making them move faster, which increases the collision frequency (total number of collisions per second).
2. More importantly, the fraction (or proportion) of molecules with energy equal to or greater than the activation energy ($\ge E_a$) increases significantly.
3. Consequently, the frequency of effective collisions increases, leading to an increase in the reaction rate.

Detailed Explanation: [DSE Full-mark Golden Framework] This is a standard 3-mark question on collision theory. The HKEAA marking scheme strictly requires the mention of "fraction" or "proportion" in point 2. If you only write "more molecules have energy greater than $E_a$", you might lose 1 mark!

Q2. 催化劑（Catalyst）能加速化學反應。試從活化能的角度解釋催化劑是如何發揮作用的，並指出催化劑在反應前後的質量有何變化。

Q2. A catalyst can speed up a chemical reaction. Explain how a catalyst functions in terms of activation energy, and state the change in its mass before and after the reaction.

查看答案及詳細解析 View Answer and Detailed Explanation

正確答案：
原理：催化劑能為反應提供另一個具有較低活化能（Lower activation energy, $E_a$）的替代途徑。這使得在相同溫度下，擁有足夠能量克服活化能屏障的分子百分比增加，從而增加了有效碰撞頻率。
質量變化：催化劑在反應前後的質量保持不變。

精修解析： DSE 必背關鍵字是「替代途徑（Alternative pathway）」和「較低活化能（Lower $E_a$）」。請注意，催化劑並不是「降低反應原本的活化能」，而是「開闢了一條新路」，這條新路的活化能比較低。另外，催化劑雖然參與了反應，但其化學性質和質量在反應前後均不改變。

Correct Answer:
Principle: A catalyst functions by providing an alternative pathway with a lower activation energy ($E_a$) for the reaction. This allows a larger fraction of molecules to have sufficient energy to overcome the energy barrier at the same temperature, thereby increasing the frequency of effective collisions.
Mass change: The mass remains unchanged before and after the reaction.

Detailed Explanation: The must-memorize DSE keywords are "alternative pathway" and "lower activation energy ($E_a$)". Note that a catalyst does not "lower the original activation energy of the reaction", but rather "opens up a new route" that happens to have a lower activation energy. Furthermore, although a catalyst participates in the reaction, its chemical properties and mass remain unchanged at the end.

Q3. 學生利用大理石碎屑（主要成分為 $CaCO_3$）與過量稀鹽酸 ($HCl$) 反應來研究反應速率。他使用電子天平記錄反應系統的質量隨時間的下降。解釋為什麼要在錐形瓶口塞上一團棉花（Cotton wool）？如果不塞棉花，測得的初始反應速率會受到什麼影響？

Q3. A student studies the rate of reaction using marble chips (mainly $CaCO_3$) and excess dilute hydrochloric acid ($HCl$). He uses an electronic balance to record the decrease in mass of the reaction system over time. Explain why a plug of cotton wool should be placed at the mouth of the conical flask. How would the measured initial reaction rate be affected if the cotton wool is omitted?

查看答案及詳細解析 View Answer and Detailed Explanation

正確答案：
塞棉花的原因：為了防止反應劇烈時產生的酸霧（或液滴）濺出（Acid spray/droplets escaping），同時容許生成的二氧化碳氣體逸出。
不塞棉花的影響：測得的初始反應速率會偏高。

精修解析： 【實驗題超級大地雷！】如果不塞棉花，逸出的除了 $CO_2$ 氣體外，還包括被氣泡帶走的酸性液滴（酸霧）。電子天平測得的質量損失會比實際單純氣體逸出時「更多、更快」，這會導致計算出來的初始反應速率偏高。答題時記得點名「酸霧」和「速率偏高」這兩個考點。

Correct Answer:
Reason for using cotton wool: To prevent acid spray (or liquid droplets) from escaping during the vigorous reaction, while still allowing the carbon dioxide gas produced to escape.
Effect of omitting it: The measured initial reaction rate would be overestimated (higher than actual).

Detailed Explanation: [Super Experimental Landmine!] If the cotton wool is omitted, what escapes is not just $CO_2$ gas but also acid droplets (acid spray) carried away by the effervescence. The mass loss measured by the electronic balance will be "more and faster" than the true gas mass loss, leading to an artificially higher calculated initial reaction rate. Remember to mention "acid spray" and "overestimated rate" in your exam answer.
