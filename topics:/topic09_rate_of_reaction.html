DSE 化學筆記：課題九 反應速率

1. 反應速率的量度方法

選擇量度方法取決於反應物及生成物的物理狀態：

  • 量度氣體體積 (Volume of gas)：使用氣筒收集。適合產生不溶於水或微溶於水氣體的反應（如 $H_2, CO_2$）。
  • 量度質量減少 (Mass loss)：將燒杯放在電子磅上。適合產生高密度氣體（如 $CO_2$）的反應。不適用於產生 $H_2$ 的反應，因為 $H_2$ 太輕，質量變化不明顯。
  • 量度透光度/沉澱物 (Disappearing cross)：如硫代硫酸鈉與稀酸反應產生黃色硫沉澱 ($S$)。
    $S_2O_3^{2-}(aq) + 2H^+(aq) \rightarrow S(s) + SO_2(g) + H_2O(l)$

2. 碰撞理論 (Collision Theory) 滿分解釋框架

解釋為何反應速率隨時間減慢：
隨着反應進行，反應物的濃度下降 $\rightarrow$ 單位體積內的粒子數目減少 $\rightarrow$ 粒子的碰撞頻率下降 $\rightarrow$ 有效碰撞頻率下降 $\rightarrow$ 速率減慢。

3. 影響反應速率的關鍵因素

因素微觀解釋 (根據碰撞理論)
濃度 (Concentration)單位體積內的反應物粒子數量增加，導致碰撞頻率增加。
固體表面積 (Surface Area)粉末提供更大的表面積，暴露更多粒子，導致碰撞頻率增加。
溫度 (Temperature)粒子平均動能增加。具有大於或等於活化能 ($E_a$) 的粒子數目顯著增加，導致有效碰撞頻率增加。
催化劑 (Catalyst)提供一條活化能 ($E_a$) 較低的另一反應途徑。更多粒子能跨越此能量門檻，導致有效碰撞頻率增加。
考試陷阱 (DSE Trap)：
加入催化劑 不會 改變反應物和生成物的焓 ($\Delta H$)，也 不會 增加生成物的總產量 (Yield)。它只能讓你更快得到相同數量的生成物。

DSE Chemistry Notes: Topic 9 Rate of Reaction

1. Methods of Measuring Reaction Rate

The choice of method depends on the physical states of reactants and products:

  • Volume of gas: Using a gas syringe. Suitable for reactions producing gases (e.g., $H_2, CO_2$).
  • Mass loss: Using an electronic balance. Suitable for reactions producing heavy gases (e.g., $CO_2$). Not suitable for $H_2$ because it is too light to show a significant mass change.
  • Turbidity / Disappearing cross: e.g., reaction between sodium thiosulphate and dilute acid producing yellow sulphur precipitate ($S$).
    $S_2O_3^{2-}(aq) + 2H^+(aq) \rightarrow S(s) + SO_2(g) + H_2O(l)$

2. Collision Theory: Perfect Answering Framework

Explaining why rate decreases over time:
As the reaction proceeds, the concentration of reactants decreases $\rightarrow$ Number of particles per unit volume decreases $\rightarrow$ Collision frequency decreases $\rightarrow$ Effective collision frequency decreases $\rightarrow$ Rate decreases.

3. Key Factors Affecting Rate

FactorMicroscopic Explanation (Collision Theory)
ConcentrationMore reactant particles per unit volume, leading to an increase in collision frequency.
Surface Area (Solid)Powder provides a larger surface area, exposing more particles, leading to an increase in collision frequency.
TemperatureAverage kinetic energy of particles increases. A larger fraction of particles possess energy $\ge$ activation energy ($E_a$), leading to a significant increase in effective collision frequency.
CatalystProvides an alternative reaction pathway with a lower activation energy ($E_a$). More particles can overcome this energy barrier, leading to an increase in effective collision frequency.
DSE Trap:
Adding a catalyst DOES NOT change the enthalpy ($\Delta H$) of reactants/products, and it DOES NOT increase the total yield of products. It only allows you to obtain the same amount of product faster.
