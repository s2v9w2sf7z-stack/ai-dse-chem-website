DSE 化學筆記：課題十三 工業化學

1. 速率方程 (Rate Equation)

對於反應 $A + B \rightarrow C$，速率方程表示為：

$$\text{Rate} = k[A]^m[B]^n$$
  • $k$ (速率常數)：只隨溫度及催化劑改變。必須小心推算其單位。
  • $m$ 和 $n$ (反應級數)：常見為 0、1 或 2。零級反應表示改變該物質濃度不會影響反應速率。

2. 阿倫尼烏斯方程與活化能 ($E_a$)

$$\ln k = \text{常數} - \frac{E_a}{R} \left(\frac{1}{T}\right)$$
DSE 圖表計算陷阱：
考評局常要求學生繪畫 $\ln k$ 對 $1/T$ 的圖表。斜率公式為：$m = -E_a / R$。
計算出的 $E_a$ 單位原本是 $J \, mol^{-1}$，作答時記得除以 1000 轉換為 $kJ \, mol^{-1}$！溫度 $T$ 必須使用絕對溫度 ($K = ^\circ C + 273$)。

3. 氯鹼工業：離子透析膜電解池

電解飽和食鹽水以產生三種重要的化工原料：

  • 陽極 (Anode) 反應：$2Cl^-(aq) \rightarrow Cl_2(g) + 2e^-$
  • 陰極 (Cathode) 反應：$2H_2O(l) + 2e^- \rightarrow H_2(g) + 2OH^-(aq)$

陽離子透析膜的兩大功用：
1. 只允許 $Na^+$ 通過以平衡電荷。
2. 阻止氯氣 ($Cl_2$) 與氫氧化鈉 ($NaOH$) 混合並發生副反應，確保產物的高純度。

4. 綠色化學 (Green Chemistry)

原子經濟 (Atom economy) 是衡量工業合成路線優劣的重要指標：

$$\text{原子經濟} = \frac{\text{目標產物的摩爾質量}}{\text{反應物的總摩爾質量}} \times 100\%$$

提升原子經濟的方法包括：選擇加成反應（Atom economy = 100%）、使用可再生資源及研發更高效的催化劑。

DSE Chemistry Notes: Topic 13 Industrial Chemistry

1. Rate Equation

For a reaction $A + B \rightarrow C$, the rate equation is expressed as:

$$\text{Rate} = k[A]^m[B]^n$$
  • $k$ (Rate constant): Changes ONLY with temperature and catalyst. Be careful when deriving its units.
  • $m$ and $n$ (Orders of reaction): Commonly 0, 1, or 2. A zero-order reaction means changing that reactant's concentration has no effect on the rate.

2. Arrhenius Equation and Activation Energy ($E_a$)

$$\ln k = \text{Constant} - \frac{E_a}{R} \left(\frac{1}{T}\right)$$
DSE Graph Calculation Traps:
HKEAA often asks students to plot $\ln k$ against $1/T$. The slope is: $m = -E_a / R$.
The calculated $E_a$ is initially in $J \, mol^{-1}$. Remember to divide by 1000 to convert to $kJ \, mol^{-1}$! Temperature $T$ must be in Kelvin ($K = ^\circ C + 273$).

3. Chlor-alkali Industry: Membrane Cell

Electrolysis of saturated brine to produce three important industrial feedstocks:

  • Anode reaction: $2Cl^-(aq) \rightarrow Cl_2(g) + 2e^-$
  • Cathode reaction: $2H_2O(l) + 2e^- \rightarrow H_2(g) + 2OH^-(aq)$

Two main functions of the Cation-permeable membrane:
1. Allows only $Na^+$ to pass through to balance the charge.
2. Prevents mixing of chlorine ($Cl_2$) and sodium hydroxide ($NaOH$) to avoid side reactions, ensuring high purity of products.

4. Green Chemistry

Atom economy is a crucial metric for evaluating the efficiency of a synthetic route:

$$\text{Atom Economy} = \frac{\text{Molar mass of desired product}}{\text{Total molar mass of reactants}} \times 100\%$$

Ways to improve atom economy include selecting addition reactions (Atom economy = 100%), using renewable resources, and developing more efficient catalysts.

✍️ 自我檢測練習（課題十三：工業化學） ✍️ Self-Assessment Quiz (Topic 13: Industrial Chemistry)

Q1. 在哈柏法（Haber Process）製氨反應中：$N_2(g) + 3H_2(g) \rightleftharpoons 2NH_3(g)$，正向反應是放熱反應（$\Delta H < 0$）。在工業生產中，科學家通常選擇約 $450^\circ C$ 的「中度高溫」而非低溫。試從反應速率（Kinetic）和化學平衡（Equilibrium）這兩個相互衝突的角度，解釋這個折衷（Compromise）生產條件的成因。

Q1. In the Haber Process for the synthesis of ammonia: $N_2(g) + 3H_2(g) \rightleftharpoons 2NH_3(g)$, the forward reaction is exothermic ($\Delta H < 0$). In industrial production, a "moderate high temperature" of about $450^\circ C$ is chosen instead of a low temperature. Explain this compromise production condition in terms of the conflicting factors of rate of reaction (kinetics) and chemical equilibrium.

查看答案及詳細解析 View Answer and Detailed Explanation

正確答案：
1. 從速率（Kinetic）角度： 較高溫度能顯著增加反應速率，使反應物分子擁有更多動能，增加有效碰撞頻率，從而縮短達到平衡所需的時間。
2. 從平衡（Equilibrium）角度： 由於正向反應是放熱的，根據勒沙特列原理，過高的溫度會使平衡向左（逆向）移動，反而降低平衡時氨的產率（Yield）。
3. 結論： 工業上採用 $450^\circ C$ 是為了在**高反應速率**與**合理的平衡產率**之間取得平衡，是一個經濟效益最大化的折衷溫度。

精修解析： 【DSE 經典 3 分工業題】這類題目是工業化學的靈魂。評卷時會分開速率點、平衡點和折衷結論點。很多同學只專注於其中一面（例如只答高溫速率快），而忽略了放熱反應在高溫下產率會下降的平衡考量。答題時必須明確分開這兩個範疇來論述，才能拿滿分數！

Correct Answer:
1. From the kinetic (rate) perspective: A higher temperature significantly increases the reaction rate. It provides reactant molecules with more kinetic energy, increasing the frequency of effective collisions, thus shortening the time required to reach equilibrium.
2. From the equilibrium perspective: Since the forward reaction is exothermic, according to Le Chatelier's Principle, an excessively high temperature shifts the equilibrium to the left, which decreases the equilibrium yield of ammonia.
3. Conclusion: The choice of $450^\circ C$ is a compromise to achieve a **high reaction rate** while maintaining a **reasonable equilibrium yield**, optimizing overall economic efficiency.

Detailed Explanation: [Classic 3-Mark Industrial Question] This type of question is central to industrial chemistry. The marking scheme allocates specific marks for the kinetic aspect, the equilibrium aspect, and the compromise conclusion. Many students only focus on one side (e.g., stating only that high temperature speeds up the reaction) while omitting the equilibrium constraint of an exothermic reaction. You must discuss both domains separately to secure full marks!

Q2. 寫出阿瑞尼斯方程（Arrhenius equation）的對數形式表達式，並說明如何透過實驗數據繪製圖表（指出 X 軸和 Y 軸代表什麼），從而求得某反應的活化能（Activation energy, $E_a$）。

Q2. State the logarithmic form of the Arrhenius equation. Explain how a graph can be plotted from experimental data (specify the X-axis and Y-axis) to determine the activation energy ($E_a$) of a reaction.

查看答案及詳細解析 View Answer and Detailed Explanation

正確答案： Correct Answer:

\ln k = -\frac{E_a}{RT} + \ln A \quad \left(\text{或 } \log k = -\frac{E_a}{2.303RT} + \log A\right)

圖表繪製與求解方法：
1. 以 $\ln k$ 或者是 $\log k$ 作為 Y 軸，以絕對溫度的倒數 $\frac{1}{T}$（單位：$K^{-1}$）作為 X 軸 繪製直線圖。
2. 該直線的斜率（Gradient, $m$）為 $-\frac{E_a}{R}$（若用 $\log$ 則為 $-\frac{E_a}{2.303R}$）。
3. 因此，活化能可經由公式計算求得：$E_a = - \text{斜率} \times R$（其中 $R$ 為理想氣體常數 $8.31 \, J \, mol^{-1} \, K^{-1}$）。

精修解析： 阿瑞尼斯方程是工業化學唯一的重頭計算題。在 DSE 卷二中，考評局非常喜歡要求學生進行「Arrhenius Plot」的線性回歸分析。緊記：溫度 $T$ 必須換算成絕對溫度克耳文（Kelvin, $K = ^\circ C + 273.15$）；利用斜率算出來的 $E_a$ 原始單位是 $J \, mol^{-1}$，在最終答案時通常要除以 1000 轉換成常見的 $kJ \, mol^{-1}$，這是一個極容易粗心扣分的地方！

Graph Plotting & Calculation Method:
1. Plot a straight-line graph with $\ln k$ (or $\log k$) on the Y-axis and the reciprocal of absolute temperature $\frac{1}{T}$ (unit: $K^{-1}$) on the X-axis.
2. The slope (gradient, $m$) of this straight line is equal to $-\frac{E_a}{R}$ (or $-\frac{E_a}{2.303R}$ if $\log$ is used).
3. Therefore, the activation energy can be determined by: $E_a = -\text{slope} \times R$ (where $R$ is the ideal gas constant $8.31 \, J \, mol^{-1} \, K^{-1}$).

Detailed Explanation: The Arrhenius equation is the primary calculation component in industrial chemistry. In DSE Paper 2, examiners frequently ask students to analyze or describe an "Arrhenius Plot". Remember: temperature $T$ MUST be converted to absolute temperature in Kelvin ($K = ^\circ C + 273.15$). The raw unit of $E_a$ obtained from the slope is $J \, mol^{-1}$; you usually need to divide it by 1000 to express the final answer in $kJ \, mol^{-1}$—a very common careless mistake trap!

Q3. 綠色化學（Green Chemistry）宣揚可持續發展的工業製程。寫出「原子經濟性（Atom economy）」的定義公式。此外，試解釋為什麼即使一個反應的「百分產率（Percentage yield）」高達 95%，其「原子經濟性」仍有可能非常低？

Q3. Green chemistry advocates for sustainable industrial processes. State the formula for defining "atom economy". Furthermore, explain why a chemical reaction might have a very low "atom economy" even if its "percentage yield" is as high as 95%.

查看答案及詳細解析 View Answer and Detailed Explanation

正確答案： Correct Answer:

\text{原子經濟性} = \frac{\text{預期產物的總公式質量}}{\text{所有反應物的總公式質量}} \times 100\%

原因解析：
1. 百分產率衡量的是實驗操作的效率（實際得到的預期產物與理論最大產量的比例）。
2. 原子經濟性則是由化學方程式的結構決定的。如果該反應在理論上會同時產生大量**沒有商業價值的副產物（By-products）**，那麼大量反應物的原子就會被浪費在副產物中。
3. 因此，即使實驗操作再完美（百分產率很高），只要反應本身產生很多廢物副產物，其原子經濟性依然會非常低。

精修解析： 【DSE 概念澄清題】百分產率與原子經濟性是兩個完全不同的維度。百分產率高只代表「你沒有倒翻溶液、反應進行得很完全」；而原子經濟性低則代表「這個化學反應的設計本質上就不夠環保/浪費資源」。綠色化學提倡使用「加成反應（Addition reaction）」，因為加成反應沒有副產物，其理論原子經濟性是完美的 100%！

Explanation:
1. Percentage yield measures the efficiency of the experimental operation (the ratio of the actual amount of desired product obtained to the theoretical maximum amount).
2. Atom economy is inherent to the chemical equation itself. If a reaction inherently generates a large amount of **unwanted/non-commercial by-products**, a significant mass of the reactant atoms will be incorporated into waste.
3. Therefore, even if the reaction is carried out perfectly with a high percentage yield, the atom economy will remain very low if the reaction pathway produces heavy by-products.

Detailed Explanation: [DSE Concept Clarification] Percentage yield and atom economy measure entirely different aspects. A high percentage yield simply means "the experimental execution was efficient and the reaction went to completion". A low atom economy means "the chemical pathway itself is fundamentally wasteful". Green chemistry highly encourages "addition reactions" because they do not generate by-products, leading to a perfect theoretical atom economy of 100%!
