DSE 化學筆記：課題十五 分析化學

1. 氧化還原滴定 (Redox Titrations)

碘-硫代硫酸鹽滴定 ($I_2 / S_2O_3^{2-}$)：這是 DSE 最常考的滴定法，常應用於測試漂白水中的次氯酸根 ($OCl^-$) 濃度或維他命C的含量。

$$I_2(aq) + 2S_2O_3^{2-}(aq) \rightarrow 2I^-(aq) + S_4O_6^{2-}(aq)$$
DSE 必考實驗細節：指示劑的使用
必須在滴定接近終點（溶液由深棕色變為淡黃色）時才加入澱粉溶液 (Starch solution)。加入後溶液變為深藍色。當最後一滴硫代硫酸鹽將所有 $I_2$ 轉化為 $I^-$ 時，溶液會突然由深藍色變為無色，這就是滴定終點。

2. 現代儀器分析：紅外光譜 (IR Spectroscopy)

用於鑑定有機分子中的官能基 (Functional groups)。在 DSE 中，你必須懂認以下兩個特徵峰：

  • $-OH$ 鍵 (醇/羧酸)：在波數約 $3200-3600 \, cm^{-1}$ 出現一個寬闊 (Broad) 的吸收峰。
  • $-C=O$ 鍵 (醛/酮/羧酸/酯)：在波數約 $1700 \, cm^{-1}$ 出現一個尖銳且強烈 (Sharp and strong) 的吸收峰。

3. 現代儀器分析：質譜 (Mass Spectrometry)

解題兩步曲：
1. 找出 分子離子峰 ($M^+$ peak)：它是光譜中最右側（m/z 最大）的特徵峰，代表化合物的相對分子質量。
2. 觀察 碎片峰 (Fragment peaks)：例如 $m/z = 15$ ($CH_3^+$), $29$ ($C_2H_5^+$), $43$ ($C_3H_7^+$ 或 $CH_3CO^+$), $45$ ($COOH^+$)。考試時必須寫出帶正電荷 ($+$) 的碎片離子式！

4. 色層分析法 (Chromatography)

用作分離混合物。物質在固定相 (Stationary phase) 和 流動相 (Mobile phase) 之間的分配差異導致分離。計算滯留因子：

$$R_f = \frac{\text{溶質移動的距離}}{\text{溶劑前沿移動的距離}}$$

DSE Chemistry Notes: Topic 15 Analytical Chemistry

1. Redox Titrations

Iodine-thiosulphate titration ($I_2 / S_2O_3^{2-}$): The most frequently tested titration in DSE, often used to determine the concentration of hypochlorite ($OCl^-$) in bleach or Vitamin C content.

$$I_2(aq) + 2S_2O_3^{2-}(aq) \rightarrow 2I^-(aq) + S_4O_6^{2-}(aq)$$
DSE Crucial Experimental Detail: Use of Indicator
Starch solution must be added only when the titration is nearing the end point (when the solution changes from dark brown to pale yellow). The solution turns dark blue. When the last drop of thiosulphate converts all $I_2$ to $I^-$, the solution suddenly changes from dark blue to colourless, marking the end point.

2. Instrumental Analysis: IR Spectroscopy

Used to identify functional groups in organic molecules. In DSE, you MUST recognize these two characteristic peaks:

  • $-OH$ bond (Alcohols/Carboxylic acids): A broad absorption peak at wavenumber around $3200-3600 \, cm^{-1}$.
  • $-C=O$ bond (Aldehydes/Ketones/Acids/Esters): A sharp and strong absorption peak at wavenumber around $1700 \, cm^{-1}$.

3. Instrumental Analysis: Mass Spectrometry (MS)

Two-step Problem Solving:
1. Find the Molecular ion peak ($M^+$ peak): The prominent peak furthest to the right (largest m/z), representing the relative molecular mass of the compound.
2. Observe Fragment peaks: E.g., $m/z = 15$ ($CH_3^+$), $29$ ($C_2H_5^+$), $43$ ($C_3H_7^+$ or $CH_3CO^+$), $45$ ($COOH^+$). In exams, you MUST write the formula of the fragment ion with a positive charge ($+$)!

4. Chromatography

Used to separate mixtures based on the partition of substances between a stationary phase and a mobile phase. Calculation of the retention factor:

$$R_f = \frac{\text{Distance travelled by solute}}{\text{Distance travelled by solvent front}}$$

✍️ 自我檢測練習（課題十五：分析化學） ✍️ Self-Assessment Quiz (Topic 15: Analytical Chemistry)

Q1. 某無色溶液中可能含有 $Al^{3+}(aq)$ 或 $Mg^{2+}(aq)$ 離子。試描述如何使用稀氫氧化鈉溶液 ($NaOH(aq)$) 作為單一試劑，透過兩個步驟的實驗來鑑別這兩種離子（寫出具體操作及預期觀察到的現象對比）。

Q1. A colourless solution may contain either $Al^{3+}(aq)$ or $Mg^{2+}(aq)$ ions. Describe how to use dilute sodium hydroxide solution ($NaOH(aq)$) as a single reagent to distinguish between these two ions through a two-step experiment (state the specific operations and comparative observations).

查看答案及詳細解析 View Answer and Detailed Explanation

正確答案：
1. 第一步（逐滴加入）： 向溶液中**逐滴**加入稀 $NaOH(aq)$。兩種離子都會反應生成白色沉澱物。
2. 第二步（加入至過量）： 繼續加入稀 $NaOH(aq)$ 直至**過量**。若白色沉澱物溶解並重新變回無色溶液，則該離子為 $Al^{3+}$；若白色沉澱物不溶解，則該離子為 $Mg^{2+}$。

精修解析： 【DSE 經典必得分大題】這題考核的是兩性（Amphoteric）金屬氫氧化物的特性。$Al(OH)_3$ 具有兩性，能溶解於過量的強鹼中，生成可溶的鋁酸根離子 $[Al(OH)_4]^-$；而 $Mg(OH)_2$ 只是常規的鹼性沉澱，不與過量鹼反應。答題時，**必須**寫出「逐滴加入（dropwise）」到「過量（in excess）」的動態操作過程，如果只寫「加 $NaOH$」，在 DSE 是無法拿滿分的！

Correct Answer:
1. Step 1 (Dropwise addition): Add dilute $NaOH(aq)$ **dropwise** to the solution. Both ions will form a white precipitate.
2. Step 2 (Addition in excess): Continue to add dilute $NaOH(aq)$ until **in excess**. If the white precipitate dissolves to form a colourless solution, the ion is $Al^{3+}$. If the white precipitate remains insoluble, the ion is $Mg^{2+}$.

Detailed Explanation: [Classic DSE Scoring Question] This question tests the property of amphoteric metal hydroxides. $Al(OH)_3$ is amphoteric and dissolves in excess strong alkali to form the soluble aluminate complex $[Al(OH)_4]^-$, whereas $Mg(OH)_2$ is a basic precipitate and does not react with excess alkali. When answering, you **must** specify the progressive procedure from "dropwise" to "in excess"; simply stating "add $NaOH$" will not yield full marks in the DSE!

Q2. 在利用薄層層析法（TLC）分離一組有機混合物時，固定相（Stationary phase）為極性的矽膠，流動相（Mobile phase）為弱極性的有機溶劑。若成分 X 的 $R_f$ 值比成分 Y 大，試從兩者與固定相及流動相的「分子間引力」角度，解釋這個現象。

Q2. In separating an organic mixture using Thin-Layer Chromatography (TLC), the stationary phase is polar silica gel and the mobile phase is a weakly polar organic solvent. If component X has a larger $R_f$ value than component Y, explain this phenomenon in terms of the intermolecular forces of both components with the stationary and mobile phases.

查看答案及詳細解析 View Answer and Detailed Explanation

正確答案：
1. $R_f$ 值越大，代表該成分隨溶劑沿層析板**移動得越快、越遠**。
2. 成分 X 的 $R_f$ 值較大，說明 X 與弱極性流動相（溶劑）之間的分子間引力較強，同時與極性固定相（矽膠）之間的分子間引力較弱（被吸附得較鬆）。
3. 相反，成分 Y 則與極性固定相有較強的引力，使其被牢牢吸附，移動得較慢，故 $R_f$ 值較小。

精修解析： 【DSE 概念地雷】層析法的核心就是「分配原理（Partition）」。答題的關鍵字一定要包含**分子間引力（Intermolecular forces）**或吸附作用（Adsorption）。緊記「物以類聚」原則：因為固定相是極性的，所以極性越強的物質（如 Y）就會跟它黏得越牢（引力強），走得越慢，導致 $R_f$ 值越小。這個邏輯在長題目中價值 2-3 分！

Correct Answer:
1. A larger $R_f$ value indicates that the component **travels faster and further** along the TLC plate with the solvent.
2. Since component X has a larger $R_f$ value, it implies that X exhibits stronger intermolecular forces with the weakly polar mobile phase (solvent) and weaker intermolecular forces with the polar stationary phase (silica gel).
3. Conversely, component Y has stronger attractive forces with the polar stationary phase, causing it to be strongly adsorbed and move slower, resulting in a smaller $R_f$ value.

Detailed Explanation: [DSE Concept Trap] The core of chromatography is the partition principle. Your answer's keywords must include **intermolecular forces** or adsorption. Remember the "like dissolves like" rule: since the stationary phase is polar, components with higher polarity (like Y) will bind more tightly to it (stronger attraction), move slower, and thus yield a smaller $R_f$ value. This logical chain is worth 2-3 marks in structured questions!

Q3. 某未知有機化合物的實驗式為 $C_2H_4O$。已知其質譜（Mass spectrum）的分子離子峰（Molecular ion peak）位於 $m/z = 88$；且其紅外光譜（IR spectrum）在 $1680-1750 \, cm^{-1}$ 處顯示出一個強烈的尖銳吸收峰，但在 $3230-3670 \, cm^{-1}$ 處沒有任何寬闊的吸收峰。試推導出該化合物的分子式，並指出它含有哪一個官能基。

Q3. An unknown organic compound has an empirical formula of $C_2H_4O$. Its mass spectrum shows a molecular ion peak at $m/z = 88$. Its IR spectrum exhibits a strong, sharp absorption peak at $1680-1750 \, cm^{-1}$, but no broad absorption peak at $3230-3670 \, cm^{-1}$. Deduce the molecular formula of the compound and identify the functional group present.

查看答案及詳細解析 View Answer and Detailed Explanation

正確答案：
分子式：$C_4H_8O_2$
官能基：羰基 (Carbonyl group, $C=O$) [或是酯基/醛基/酮基，但在此題分子式下，由於排除了 $-OH$，它很可能是酯或醛酮，寫羰基最安全]

推導步驟解析：
1. 求分子式： 實驗式 $C_2H_4O$ 的公式質量 $= 12.0 \times 2 + 1.0 \times 4 + 16.0 = 44$。質譜中的分子離子峰 $m/z = 88$ 代表該化合物的相對分子質量為 88。因為 $\frac{88}{44} = 2$，所以分子式為 $(C_2H_4O) \times 2 = C_4H_8O_2$。
2. 解讀紅外光譜： 在 $1680-1750 \, cm^{-1}$ 處的強烈尖銳峰對應的是 **$C=O$ 鍵（羰基）** 的伸縮振動。而在 $3230-3670 \, cm^{-1}$ 處沒有寬峰，明確排除了 **$O-H$ 鍵（醇或羧酸）** 的存在。結合含有兩個氧原子且沒有 $-OH$ 的特徵，該化合物極可能是一個「酯（Ester）」。

精修解析： 【DSE 譜圖題必殺技】現代儀器分析題就像偵探破案。質譜的分子離子峰（最後面那個顯著的峰）是用來鎖定**最精確的分子質量**的。而紅外光譜則是用來提供**官能基的情報**。在答題時，一定要引用數據（如提及 $1680-1750 \, cm^{-1}$ 代表 $C=O$），這是考評局在數據分析題目上的硬性扣分點！

Correct Answer:
Molecular formula: $C_4H_8O_2$
Functional group: Carbonyl group ($C=O$) [or ester group]

Deduction Steps:
1. Find molecular formula: The formula mass of the empirical formula $C_2H_4O$ is $12.0 \times 2 + 1.0 \times 4 + 16.0 = 44$. The molecular ion peak at $m/z = 88$ in the mass spectrum indicates that the relative molecular mass of the compound is 88. Since $\frac{88}{44} = 2$, the molecular formula is $(C_2H_4O) \times 2 = C_4H_8O_2$.
2. Interpret IR spectrum: The strong, sharp peak at $1680-1750 \, cm^{-1}$ corresponds to the stretching vibration of the **$C=O$ bond (carbonyl group)**. The absence of a broad peak at $3230-3670 \, cm^{-1}$ explicitly rules out the **$O-H$ bond (alcohol or carboxylic acid)**. Given that it contains two oxygen atoms but no $-OH$ group, the compound is highly likely an ester.

Detailed Explanation: [DSE Spectrum Problem Strategy] Solving instrumental analysis questions is like detective work. The molecular ion peak in the mass spectrum (the significant peak furthest to the right) is used to lock down the **exact relative molecular mass**. Meanwhile, the IR spectrum provides **functional group clues**. When answering, always quote the specific wavenumber ranges (e.g., stating that $1680-1750 \, cm^{-1}$ indicates $C=O$), as failing to cite data is a common reason for mark loss!
