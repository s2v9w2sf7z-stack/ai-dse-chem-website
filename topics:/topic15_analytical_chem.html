DSE 化學筆記：課題十五 分析化學

1. 氧化還原滴定 (Redox Titrations)

碘-硫代硫酸鹽滴定 ($I_2 / S_2O_3^{2-}$)：這是 DSE 最常考的滴定法，常應用於測試漂白水中的次氯酸根 ($OCl^-$) 濃度或維他命C的含量。

$$I_2(aq) + 2S_2O_3^{2-}(aq) \rightarrow 2I^-(aq) + S_4O_6^{2-}(aq)$$
DSE 必考實驗細節：指示劑的使用
必須在滴定接近終點（溶液由深棕色變為淡黃色）時才加入澱粉溶液 (Starch solution)。加入後溶液變為深藍色。當最後一滴硫代硫酸鹽將所有 $I_2$ 轉化為 $I^-$ 時，溶液會突然由深藍色變為無色，這就是滴定終點。

2. 現代儀器分析：紅外光譜 (IR Spectroscopy)

用於鑑定有機分子中的官能基 (Functional groups)。在 DSE 中，你必須懂認以下兩個特徵峰：

  • $-OH$ 鍵 (醇/羧酸)：在波數約 $3200-3600 \, cm^{-1}$ 出現一個寬闊 (Broad) 的吸收峰。
  • $-C=O$ 鍵 (醛/酮/羧酸/酯)：在波數約 $1700 \, cm^{-1}$ 出現一個尖銳且強烈 (Sharp and strong) 的吸收峰。

3. 現代儀器分析：質譜 (Mass Spectrometry)

解題兩步曲：
1. 找出 分子離子峰 ($M^+$ peak)：它是光譜中最右側（m/z 最大）的特徵峰，代表化合物的相對分子質量。
2. 觀察 碎片峰 (Fragment peaks)：例如 $m/z = 15$ ($CH_3^+$), $29$ ($C_2H_5^+$), $43$ ($C_3H_7^+$ 或 $CH_3CO^+$), $45$ ($COOH^+$)。考試時必須寫出帶正電荷 ($+$) 的碎片離子式！

4. 色層分析法 (Chromatography)

用作分離混合物。物質在固定相 (Stationary phase) 和 流動相 (Mobile phase) 之間的分配差異導致分離。計算滯留因子：

$$R_f = \frac{\text{溶質移動的距離}}{\text{溶劑前沿移動的距離}}$$

DSE Chemistry Notes: Topic 15 Analytical Chemistry

1. Redox Titrations

Iodine-thiosulphate titration ($I_2 / S_2O_3^{2-}$): The most frequently tested titration in DSE, often used to determine the concentration of hypochlorite ($OCl^-$) in bleach or Vitamin C content.

$$I_2(aq) + 2S_2O_3^{2-}(aq) \rightarrow 2I^-(aq) + S_4O_6^{2-}(aq)$$
DSE Crucial Experimental Detail: Use of Indicator
Starch solution must be added only when the titration is nearing the end point (when the solution changes from dark brown to pale yellow). The solution turns dark blue. When the last drop of thiosulphate converts all $I_2$ to $I^-$, the solution suddenly changes from dark blue to colourless, marking the end point.

2. Instrumental Analysis: IR Spectroscopy

Used to identify functional groups in organic molecules. In DSE, you MUST recognize these two characteristic peaks:

  • $-OH$ bond (Alcohols/Carboxylic acids): A broad absorption peak at wavenumber around $3200-3600 \, cm^{-1}$.
  • $-C=O$ bond (Aldehydes/Ketones/Acids/Esters): A sharp and strong absorption peak at wavenumber around $1700 \, cm^{-1}$.

3. Instrumental Analysis: Mass Spectrometry (MS)

Two-step Problem Solving:
1. Find the Molecular ion peak ($M^+$ peak): The prominent peak furthest to the right (largest m/z), representing the relative molecular mass of the compound.
2. Observe Fragment peaks: E.g., $m/z = 15$ ($CH_3^+$), $29$ ($C_2H_5^+$), $43$ ($C_3H_7^+$ or $CH_3CO^+$), $45$ ($COOH^+$). In exams, you MUST write the formula of the fragment ion with a positive charge ($+$)!

4. Chromatography

Used to separate mixtures based on the partition of substances between a stationary phase and a mobile phase. Calculation of the retention factor:

$$R_f = \frac{\text{Distance travelled by solute}}{\text{Distance travelled by solvent front}}$$
