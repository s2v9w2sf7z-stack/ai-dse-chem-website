DSE 化學筆記：課題八 化學反應和能量

1. 系統與環境的熱能轉移

放熱反應 (Exothermic)：反應物總焓 > 生成物總焓。
熱能流向：系統 $\rightarrow$ 環境。環境溫度上升。 $\Delta H < 0$（負值）。
吸熱反應 (Endothermic)：反應物總焓 < 生成物總焓。
熱能流向：環境 $\rightarrow$ 系統。環境溫度下降。 $\Delta H > 0$（正值）。

2. 量熱法 (Calorimetry) 計算公式

計算反應釋放或吸收的熱能 ($E$)：

$$E = mc\Delta T$$
  • $m$：被加熱/冷卻物質的質量 (g)（通常是水的質量）
  • $c$：比熱容量 (如水為 $4.2 \, J \, g^{-1} \, K^{-1}$)
  • $\Delta T$：溫度變化 ($K$ 或 $^\circ C$)

計算摩爾焓變 ($\Delta H$)：

$$\Delta H = -\frac{E}{n}$$

注意：若溫度上升（放熱），加上負號以符合 $\Delta H$ 為負值的定義。

3. 赫斯定律 (Hess's Law)

「化學反應的總焓變只取決於始態和終態，與反應途徑無關。」

DSE 常考公式推導：

  • 利用燃燒焓 ($\Delta H_c^\ominus$) 計算：
    $\Delta H = \Sigma \Delta H_c^\ominus (\text{反應物}) - \Sigma \Delta H_c^\ominus (\text{生成物})$
  • 利用生成焓 ($\Delta H_f^\ominus$) 計算：
    $\Delta H = \Sigma \Delta H_f^\ominus (\text{生成物}) - \Sigma \Delta H_f^\ominus (\text{反應物})$

DSE Chemistry Notes: Topic 8 Chemical Reactions and Energy

1. Heat Transfer between System and Surroundings

Exothermic reaction: Total enthalpy of reactants > products.
Heat flow: System $\rightarrow$ Surroundings. Surrounding temperature rises. $\Delta H < 0$ (negative).
Endothermic reaction: Total enthalpy of reactants < products.
Heat flow: Surroundings $\rightarrow$ System. Surrounding temperature drops. $\Delta H > 0$ (positive).

2. Calorimetry Calculations

Calculate heat transferred ($E$):

$$E = mc\Delta T$$
  • $m$: mass of substance heated/cooled (g) (usually water)
  • $c$: specific heat capacity (e.g., water is $4.2 \, J \, g^{-1} \, K^{-1}$)
  • $\Delta T$: temperature change ($K$ or $^\circ C$)

Calculate molar enthalpy change ($\Delta H$):

$$\Delta H = -\frac{E}{n}$$

Note: Add a negative sign if temperature rises (exothermic) to match the negative $\Delta H$ definition.

3. Hess's Law

"The total enthalpy change of a chemical reaction is independent of the pathway between the initial and final states."

Common DSE Formulae:

  • Using Enthalpy change of combustion ($\Delta H_c^\ominus$):
    $\Delta H = \Sigma \Delta H_c^\ominus (\text{Reactants}) - \Sigma \Delta H_c^\ominus (\text{Products})$
  • Using Enthalpy change of formation ($\Delta H_f^\ominus$):
    $\Delta H = \Sigma \Delta H_f^\ominus (\text{Products}) - \Sigma \Delta H_f^\ominus (\text{Reactants})$

✍️ 自我檢測練習（課題八：化學反應和能量） ✍️ Self-Assessment Quiz (Topic 8: Chemical Reactions and Energy)

Q1. 許多化學反應會伴隨能量變化。試從「鍵斷裂（Bond breaking）」和「鍵形成（Bond forming）」的角度，解釋為什麼某些反應是放熱反應（Exothermic reaction）。

Q1. Many chemical reactions are accompanied by energy changes. In terms of "bond breaking" and "bond forming", explain why some reactions are exothermic reactions.

查看答案及詳細解析 View Answer and Detailed Explanation

正確答案： 斷裂反應物中的化學鍵需要吸收能量，而形成生成物中的新化學鍵會釋放能量。在放熱反應中，新鍵形成所釋放的能量大於舊鍵斷裂所吸收的能量，因此淨能量以熱的形式釋放到周圍環境。

精修解析： 這是 DSE 必背的基礎解釋題（常考 2 分）。答題時必須明確指出兩個對稱的過程：「斷鍵吸熱」和「結鍵放熱」，並進行大小比較。很多同學只寫「釋放的能量大於吸收的能量」，漏掉了具體提「鍵（Bonds）」，會被考評局扣分。

Correct Answer: Breaking chemical bonds in reactants requires absorbing energy, while forming new chemical bonds in products releases energy. In an exothermic reaction, the energy released from bond forming is greater than the energy absorbed in bond breaking. Thus, net energy is released to the surroundings as heat.

Detailed Explanation: This is a must-memorize explanation question in DSE (usually worth 2 marks). The answer must clearly state two symmetrical processes: "bond breaking absorbs heat" and "bond forming releases heat", followed by a comparison. Many students only write "energy released is greater than energy absorbed" without specifying "bonds", which often leads to mark deduction.

Q2. 稀氫氯酸 ($HCl(aq)$) 與稀氫氧化鈉溶液 ($NaOH(aq)$) 反應的標準中和焓變為 $\Delta H = -57.3 \, kJ \, mol^{-1}$。然而，若用相同濃度的稀乙酸 ($CH_3COOH(aq)$) 代替氫氯酸進行實驗，測得的焓變數值會較小（即釋放較少熱量，例如 $-55.2 \, kJ \, mol^{-1}$）。試解釋這個差異。

Q2. The standard enthalpy change of neutralisation between dilute hydrochloric acid ($HCl(aq)$) and dilute sodium hydroxide solution ($NaOH(aq)$) is $\Delta H = -57.3 \, kJ \, mol^{-1}$. However, if dilute ethanoic acid ($CH_3COOH(aq)$) of the same concentration is used instead of hydrochloric acid, the measured value becomes less exothermic (i.e. releases less heat, e.g., $-55.2 \, kJ \, mol^{-1}$). Explain this difference.

查看答案及詳細解析 View Answer and Detailed Explanation

正確答案： 乙酸是一種弱酸，在水中只會部分電離 (Partially ionize)，大部分仍以酸分子形式存在。在中和反應過程中，中和所釋放出的部分熱能，會被用於將未電離的乙酸分子完全電離（以克服分子內的共價鍵）。因此，最終淨釋放的熱量會較少。

精修解析： 【DSE 每年必考大熱門！】中和反應的本質是 $H^+(aq) + OH^-(aq) \rightarrow H_2O(l)$。強酸強鹼完全電離，所以中和熱恆定為 $-57.3 \, kJ \, mol^{-1}$。弱酸（或弱鹼）因為要「先借用一部分能量來給自己完全電離」，所以最後散發出來的熱量就被「扣減」了。這個概念不論在長問答還是 MC 題都極高頻出現！

Correct Answer: Ethanoic acid is a weak acid which only partially ionizes in water; most of it exists as molecules. During the neutralisation process, part of the heat energy released from the reaction is absorbed/used to completely ionize the remaining unionized ethanoic acid molecules (to overcome covalent bonds). Therefore, the net heat released is lower.

Detailed Explanation: [DSE Perennial Hot Topic!] The essence of neutralisation is $H^+(aq) + OH^-(aq) \rightarrow H_2O(l)$. Strong acids and strong bases ionize completely, so their enthalpy change is always $-57.3 \, kJ \, mol^{-1}$. Weak acids (or weak bases) need to "borrow some energy first to complete their own ionization", meaning the final heat given out is "deducted". This concept is highly frequent in both structured and MC questions!

Q3. 簡述赫斯定律（Hess's Law）的核心內容。並回答：若反應途徑 A $\rightarrow$ B 的焓變為 $\Delta H_1$，途徑 B $\rightarrow$ C 的焓變為 $\Delta H_2$，那麼直接由 A $\rightarrow$ C 的焓變 $\Delta H_3$ 應該如何用 $\Delta H_1$ 和 $\Delta H_2$ 表示？

Q3. Briefly state the core principle of Hess's Law. If the enthalpy change for the pathway A $\rightarrow$ B is $\Delta H_1$, and for B $\rightarrow$ C is $\Delta H_2$, how should the enthalpy change $\Delta H_3$ for the direct pathway A $\rightarrow$ C be expressed in terms of $\Delta H_1$ and $\Delta H_2$?

查看答案及詳細解析 View Answer and Detailed Explanation

正確答案：
赫斯定律內容：一個化學反應的總焓變只與反應的始態（反應物）和終態（生成物）有關，而與反應所經過的途徑無關。
數學表達式：

Correct Answer:
Hess's Law states that the overall enthalpy change of a chemical reaction is dependent only on the initial state (reactants) and final state (products), and is independent of the pathway taken.
Mathematical expression:

ΔH₃ = ΔH₁ + ΔH₂

精修解析： 赫斯定律是能量守恆定律在化學中的應用。它允許我們通過已知的反應（例如燃燒焓變 $\Delta H_c$ 或生成焓變 $\Delta H_f$），利用「能量循環圖（Enthalpy cycle）」間接計算出一些在實驗室很難直接測量的反應焓變。做題時，緊記「順著箭頭箭頭相加，逆著箭頭符號變反」的循環計算口訣！

Detailed Explanation: Hess's Law is an application of the Law of Conservation of Energy in chemistry. It allows us to indirectly calculate the enthalpy changes of reactions that are difficult to measure directly in the laboratory, using known reactions (such as enthalpy change of combustion $\Delta H_c$ or formation $\Delta H_f$) via an "enthalpy cycle". When solving calculation problems, remember the golden rule: "add up enthalpies along the arrow directions, reverse the sign if moving against the arrow direction"!
