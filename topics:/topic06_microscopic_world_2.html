1. VSEPR 理論與分子形狀

中心原子的電子對（包括鍵合電子對和孤電子對）會互相排斥，並傾向相距最遠。孤電子對的排斥力最強，會「擠壓」鍵角。

2. 極性 (Polarity)

分子極性的判斷兩步曲：
1. 分子內是否有極性鍵？（視乎兩原子的電負性差異）。
2. 分子的立體形狀是否對稱？若對稱（如 $CCl_4$, $CO_2$），各極性鍵的偶極矩會互相抵消，整個分子屬於非極性 (Non-polar)。若不對稱（如 $H_2O$, $NH_3$, $CHCl_3$），偶極矩無法抵消，分子屬於極性 (Polar)。

3. 分子間引力與物理性質

  • 范德華力 (Van der Waals' forces)：所有分子間都有。分子體積越大（電子越多），范德華力越強。
  • 氫鍵 (Hydrogen bond)：當氫原子與電負性極高的 $F$、$O$ 或 $N$ 原子鍵合時產生。含有氫鍵的物質（如水、醇類）具有異常高的沸點。
  • 冰的密度：水結冰時，分子透過氫鍵形成開放式六角形結構，分子間留下大量空隙，導致體積膨脹，密度小於液態水。

DSE Chemistry Notes: Topic 6 Microscopic World II

1. VSEPR Theory and Molecular Shapes

Electron pairs (both bond pairs and lone pairs) around a central atom repel each other and tend to stay as far apart as possible. Lone pairs exert greater repulsion and "squeeze" the bond angles.

Molecule ExampleBond Pairs on Central AtomLone PairsShapeBond Angle
Methane ($CH_4$)40Tetrahedral109.5°
Ammonia ($NH_3$)31Trigonal pyramidal107°
Water ($H_2O$)22V-shaped / Bent104.5°
Carbon dioxide ($CO_2$)2 (treat double bond as 1 unit)0Linear180°

2. Polarity

Two Steps to Determine Molecular Polarity:
1. Are there polar bonds? (Depends on the difference in electronegativity).
2. Is the molecular shape symmetrical? If symmetrical (e.g., $CCl_4$, $CO_2$), the dipole moments of polar bonds cancel out each other, making the molecule non-polar. If asymmetrical (e.g., $H_2O$, $NH_3$, $CHCl_3$), they do not cancel, and the molecule is polar.

3. Intermolecular Forces and Physical Properties

  • Van der Waals' forces: Exist between all molecules. The larger the molecular size (more electrons), the stronger the forces.
  • Hydrogen bonds: Form when a hydrogen atom is bonded to a highly electronegative atom ($F$, $O$, or $N$). Substances with hydrogen bonding (e.g., water, alcohols) have exceptionally high boiling points.
  • Density of Ice: When water freezes, molecules form an open hexagonal structure held by hydrogen bonds. The large empty spaces increase volume, making ice less dense than liquid water.

Q1. 預測氨分子 ($NH_3$) 的立體形狀和約莫的鍵角，並利用價層電子對排斥理論 (VSEPR theory) 簡述原因。

Q1. Predict the shape and approximate bond angle of an ammonia molecule ($NH_3$), and briefly explain your answer using the Valence Shell Electron Pair Repulsion (VSEPR) theory.

查看答案及詳細解析 View Answer and Detailed Explanation

正確答案： 立體形狀為三角錐體 (Trigonal pyramidal)；鍵角約為 $107^\circ$。

原因解析： 在 $NH_3$ 中，中心氮原子最外層有 5 個電子，與 3 個氫原子形成 3 對成鍵電子對 (bonding pairs)，並剩下一對孤餘電子對 (lone pair)。根據 VSEPR 理論，這 4 對電子對會盡量遠離以減低排斥力（呈四面體排列）。由於「孤對-鍵對」的排斥力大於「鍵對-鍵對」的排斥力，孤餘電子對會把三個 $N-H$ 鍵向下壓，使形狀變成三角錐體，鍵角亦由理想四面體的 $109.5^\circ$ 縮小至約 $107^\circ$。

Correct Answer: Shape is trigonal pyramidal; bond angle is approximately $107^\circ$.

Explanation: In $NH_3$, the central nitrogen atom has 5 outermost electrons. It shares electrons with 3 hydrogen atoms to form 3 bonding pairs, leaving 1 lone pair of electrons. According to VSEPR theory, these 4 electron pairs arrange themselves as far apart as possible to minimize repulsion (in a tetrahedral arrangement). Since lone pair-bonding pair repulsion is stronger than bonding pair-bonding pair repulsion, the lone pair pushes the three $N-H$ bonds closer together, resulting in a trigonal pyramidal shape and reducing the bond angle from the ideal $109.5^\circ$ to about $107^\circ$.

Q2. 四氯化碳 ($CCl_4$) 分子內含有四個極性的 $C-Cl$ 鍵，但為什麼整個 $CCl_4$ 分子卻是非極性 (Non-polar) 的？

Q2. Tetrachloride ($CCl_4$) contains four polar $C-Cl$ bonds, but why is the $CCl_4$ molecule as a whole non-polar?

查看答案及詳細解析 View Answer and Detailed Explanation

正確答案： 因為四氯化碳具有高度對稱的正四面體 (Tetrahedral)形狀。四個 $C-Cl$ 鍵的鍵偶極矩 (bond dipoles) 大小相等且方向相反，彼此完全抵消 (cancel out each other completely)，因此整個分子沒有淨偶極矩 (no net dipole moment)。

精修解析： 【DSE 滿分標準句式】區分「鍵的極性」和「分子極性」是必考題。拿滿分的關鍵在於寫出：1. 分子形狀具有「對稱性」；2. 鍵偶極矩「完全抵消」。如果只寫「形狀對稱」而沒提到「偶極矩抵消」，通常會被扣掉 1 分。

Correct Answer: Because $CCl_4$ has a highly symmetrical tetrahedral shape. The bond dipoles of the four polar $C-Cl$ bonds are equal in magnitude and point in opposite directions, thus cancelling out each other completely. As a result, the molecule has no net dipole moment.

Detailed Explanation: [DSE Full-mark Standard Formula] Distinguishing between "bond polarity" and "molecular polarity" is a must-test. The keys to full marks are: 1. State that the molecular shape is "symmetrical"; 2. State that the bond dipoles "cancel out completely". If you only mention symmetry without mentioning the cancellation of dipoles, you will usually lose 1 mark.

Q3. 水 ($H_2O$) 的相對分子質量（18）小於硫化氫 ($H_2S$，相對分子質量為 34），但水的沸點（100°C）卻遠高於硫化氫（-60°C）。試解釋這個現象。

Q3. Water ($H_2O$) has a smaller relative molecular mass (18) than hydrogen sulphide ($H_2S$, RMM = 34), but the boiling point of water (100°C) is much higher than that of hydrogen sulphide (-60°C). Explain this phenomenon.

查看答案及詳細解析 View Answer and Detailed Explanation

正確答案： 水分子之間存在著強大的氫鍵 (Hydrogen bonds)，因為水含有與電負性極高的氧原子相連的氫原子（具有 $O-H$ 鍵）。而硫化氫分子之間只有較弱的范德華力 (Van der Waals' forces)。克服水分子間的氫鍵需要比克服硫化氫分子間的范德華力多得多的熱能，因此水的沸點遠高於硫化氫。

精修解析： 【DSE 核心大題】比較沸點時，一定要明確指出**每一種物質各自擁有什麼分子間引力**。本題的常見錯誤是漏掉提及 $H_2S$ 的「范德華力」，或誤以為 $H_2S$ 也有氫鍵。緊記只有當 $H$ 原子直接連上 $F, O, N$ 這三個超強電負性原子時，分子間才會出現氫鍵！

Correct Answer: Strong hydrogen bonds exist between water molecules because water contains hydrogen atoms bonded to a highly electronegative oxygen atom ($O-H$ bond). In contrast, only weaker Van der Waals' forces exist between hydrogen sulphide molecules. Much more heat energy is required to overcome the hydrogen bonds between water molecules than the Van der Waals' forces between $H_2S$ molecules, resulting in a much higher boiling point for water.

Detailed Explanation: [DSE Core Long Question] When comparing boiling points, you must explicitly state **what type of intermolecular force exists in each substance**. A common mistake here is omitting the mention of "Van der Waals' forces" for $H_2S$, or mistakenly assuming $H_2S$ has hydrogen bonds. Remember, hydrogen bonding only occurs when an $H$ atom is directly bonded to $F, O, \text{or } N$!
