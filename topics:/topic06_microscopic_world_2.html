1. VSEPR 理論與分子形狀

中心原子的電子對（包括鍵合電子對和孤電子對）會互相排斥，並傾向相距最遠。孤電子對的排斥力最強，會「擠壓」鍵角。

2. 極性 (Polarity)

分子極性的判斷兩步曲：
1. 分子內是否有極性鍵？（視乎兩原子的電負性差異）。
2. 分子的立體形狀是否對稱？若對稱（如 $CCl_4$, $CO_2$），各極性鍵的偶極矩會互相抵消，整個分子屬於非極性 (Non-polar)。若不對稱（如 $H_2O$, $NH_3$, $CHCl_3$），偶極矩無法抵消，分子屬於極性 (Polar)。

3. 分子間引力與物理性質

  • 范德華力 (Van der Waals' forces)：所有分子間都有。分子體積越大（電子越多），范德華力越強。
  • 氫鍵 (Hydrogen bond)：當氫原子與電負性極高的 $F$、$O$ 或 $N$ 原子鍵合時產生。含有氫鍵的物質（如水、醇類）具有異常高的沸點。
  • 冰的密度：水結冰時，分子透過氫鍵形成開放式六角形結構，分子間留下大量空隙，導致體積膨脹，密度小於液態水。

DSE Chemistry Notes: Topic 6 Microscopic World II

1. VSEPR Theory and Molecular Shapes

Electron pairs (both bond pairs and lone pairs) around a central atom repel each other and tend to stay as far apart as possible. Lone pairs exert greater repulsion and "squeeze" the bond angles.

2. Polarity

Two Steps to Determine Molecular Polarity:
1. Are there polar bonds? (Depends on the difference in electronegativity).
2. Is the molecular shape symmetrical? If symmetrical (e.g., $CCl_4$, $CO_2$), the dipole moments of polar bonds cancel out each other, making the molecule non-polar. If asymmetrical (e.g., $H_2O$, $NH_3$, $CHCl_3$), they do not cancel, and the molecule is polar.

3. Intermolecular Forces and Physical Properties

  • Van der Waals' forces: Exist between all molecules. The larger the molecular size (more electrons), the stronger the forces.
  • Hydrogen bonds: Form when a hydrogen atom is bonded to a highly electronegative atom ($F$, $O$, or $N$). Substances with hydrogen bonding (e.g., water, alcohols) have exceptionally high boiling points.
  • Density of Ice: When water freezes, molecules form an open hexagonal structure held by hydrogen bonds. The large empty spaces increase volume, making ice less dense than liquid water.
