DSE 化學筆記：課題三 金屬

1. 金屬的提取與活動性序列

金屬的提取方法取決於其在金屬活動性序中的位置。活潑性越高的金屬，其化合物越穩定，提取所需的能量就越大。

金屬位置提取方法實例與化學反應
高活潑性 ($K$ 至 $Al$)電解熔融礦石電解熔融氧化鋁以提取鋁 ($Al$)。
中活潑性 ($Zn$ 至 $Cu$)與碳共熱（碳還原法）$2Fe_2O_3(s) + 3C(s) \rightarrow 4Fe(s) + 3CO_2(g)$
低活潑性 ($Hg, Ag$)單獨加熱礦石$2HgO(s) \rightarrow 2Hg(l) + O_2(g)$

2. 金屬的化學反應與置換

A. 與水及酸反應

  • 鈉與冷水劇烈反應：
    $2Na(s) + 2H_2O(l) \rightarrow 2NaOH(aq) + H_2(g)$
  • 鎂與蒸汽反應：
    $Mg(s) + H_2O(g) \rightarrow MgO(s) + H_2(g)$

B. 置換反應 (Displacement Reaction)

較活潑的金屬能將較不活潑的金屬從其鹽溶液中置換出來。例如將鐵釘放入硫酸銅($II$)溶液中：

  • 實驗現象：藍色溶液漸變成淡綠色（因形成 $Fe^{2+}$）；鐵釘表面覆蓋一層紅褐色固體（因置換出 $Cu$）。
  • 離子方程式：
    $Fe(s) + Cu^{2+}(aq) \rightarrow Fe^{2+}(aq) + Cu(s)$

3. 鐵的生鏽與防鏽

鐵生鏽的必要條件是水和氧氣。防鏽的核心原理是隔絕這兩者，或利用電化學原理。

犧牲性保護 (Sacrificial Protection)：當鐵連接到較活潑的金屬（如 $Zn$）時，較活潑的金屬會優先失去電子而被氧化，從而保護鐵不被生鏽。即使表面有刮痕，此保護依然有效。

DSE Chemistry Notes: Topic 3 Metals

1. Metal Extraction and Reactivity Series

The extraction method of a metal depends on its position in the metal reactivity series. More reactive metals form more stable compounds, requiring more energy to extract.

Position in SeriesExtraction MethodExamples & Chemical Equations
High reactivity ($K$ to $Al$)Electrolysis of molten oreElectrolysis of molten aluminium oxide to extract Al.
Medium reactivity ($Zn$ to $Cu$)Heating with carbon (Carbon reduction)$2Fe_2O_3(s) + 3C(s) \rightarrow 4Fe(s) + 3CO_2(g)$
Low reactivity ($Hg, Ag$)Heating ore alone$2HgO(s) \rightarrow 2Hg(l) + O_2(g)$

2. Chemical Reactions and Displacement

A. Reactions with Water and Acids

  • Sodium reacts vigorously with cold water:
    $2Na(s) + 2H_2O(l) \rightarrow 2NaOH(aq) + H_2(g)$
  • Magnesium reacts with steam:
    $Mg(s) + H_2O(g) \rightarrow MgO(s) + H_2(g)$

B. Displacement Reactions

A more reactive metal displaces a less reactive metal from its salt solution. E.g., placing an iron nail into copper(II) sulphate solution:

  • Observations: Blue solution turns pale green (due to $Fe^{2+}$); reddish-brown solid deposits on the nail (due to $Cu$).
  • Ionic Equation:
    $Fe(s) + Cu^{2+}(aq) \rightarrow Fe^{2+}(aq) + Cu(s)$

3. Iron Corrosion (Rusting) and Prevention

The essential conditions for rusting are water and oxygen. Prevention relies on breaking this contact or electrochemistry.

Sacrificial Protection: When iron is connected to a more reactive metal (e.g., $Zn$), the more reactive metal loses electrons preferentially and undergoes oxidation, protecting the iron. This works even if the surface is scratched.

Q1. 鎂 ($Mg$) 與冷水反應極其緩慢，但與蒸汽反應卻非常劇烈。寫出鎂與蒸汽反應的化學方程式（需包含物態符號），並指出這個反應與「鎂與冷水反應」在含鎂生成物上的主要分別。

Q1. Magnesium ($Mg$) reacts very slowly with cold water but vigorously with steam. Write the chemical equation for the reaction between magnesium and steam (including state symbols), and state the main difference in the magnesium-containing product compared to the reaction with cold water.

查看答案及詳細解析 View Answer and Detailed Explanation

正確答案： Correct Answer:

Mg (s) + H₂O (g) → MgO (s) + H₂ (g)

分別： 與蒸汽反應會生成氧化鎂 ($MgO$)，而與冷水反應則會生成氫氧化鎂 ($Mg(OH)_2$)。

精修解析： 這是一個極常見的 DSE 陷阱題！很多同學會忘記寫 $H_2O$ 的狀態符號是 $(g)$（代表蒸汽），或是錯誤地把產物寫成氫氧化鎂。緊記：活潑金屬與冷水產生「氫氧化物」，與蒸汽則產生「氧化物」。

Difference: The reaction with steam produces magnesium oxide ($MgO$), whereas the reaction with cold water produces magnesium hydroxide ($Mg(OH)_2$).

Detailed Explanation: This is a very common DSE trap! Many students forget to write the state symbol $(g)$ for $H_2O$ (representing steam), or mistakenly write the product as magnesium hydroxide. Remember: reactive metals produce "hydroxides" with cold water, but "oxides" with steam.

Q2. 將一塊鋅片 ($Zn$) 放入硫酸銅(II)溶液 ($CuSO_4(aq)$) 中。描述兩項預期會觀察到的實驗現象，並寫出相關的離子方程式。

Q2. A piece of zinc ($Zn$) is placed into a copper(II) sulphate solution ($CuSO_4(aq)$). Describe TWO expected experimental observations and write the relevant ionic equation.

查看答案及詳細解析 View Answer and Detailed Explanation

正確答案：
實驗現象：
1. 藍色溶液漸漸變為無色（或顏色變淡）。
2. 鋅片表面有紅褐色固體沉澱。

離子方程式：

Correct Answer:
Observations:
1. The blue solution gradually becomes colourless (or fades).
2. A reddish-brown solid deposits on the zinc.

Ionic Equation:

Zn (s) + Cu²⁺ (aq) → Zn²⁺ (aq) + Cu (s)

精修解析： 因為鋅在金屬活動性序中比銅高，所以能把銅從溶液中置換出來。藍色的 $Cu^{2+}$ 離子被消耗並轉化為紅褐色的銅金屬 ($Cu$)，同時生成無色的 $Zn^{2+}$ 離子。寫離子方程式時，不需要寫出沒有參與反應的硫酸根離子 ($SO_4^{2-}$)。

Detailed Explanation: Since zinc is higher than copper in the metal reactivity series, it can displace copper from the solution. The blue $Cu^{2+}$ ions are consumed and converted into reddish-brown copper metal ($Cu$), while colourless $Zn^{2+}$ ions are formed. When writing the ionic equation, spectator ions like sulphate ions ($SO_4^{2-}$) should be omitted.

Q3. 在地下鐵管表面連接鎂塊 ($Mg$) 可以有效防止鐵管生鏽。說出這種防鏽方法的名稱，並從電子轉移的角度解釋其原理。

Q3. Attaching magnesium ($Mg$) blocks to the surface of underground iron pipes can effectively prevent the iron from rusting. Name this method of rust prevention and explain its principle in terms of electron transfer.

查看答案及詳細解析 View Answer and Detailed Explanation

正確答案：
方法名稱：犧牲性保護 (Sacrificial protection)。
原理：因為鎂比鐵活潑，鎂會優先失去電子（被氧化）。釋放出的電子會流向鐵，從而防止鐵失去電子（即防止鐵被氧化或生鏽）。

精修解析： DSE 極高頻的解釋題！答題關鍵字是「較活潑金屬」、「優先失去電子」。只要鐵不斷接收到電子，它就無法變成 $Fe^{2+}$ 離子，因此生鏽過程無法發生。這也解釋了為何即使塗層被刮破，鍍鋅鐵（馬口鐵）依然能防鏽。

Correct Answer:
Method: Sacrificial protection.
Principle: Since magnesium is more reactive than iron, magnesium will preferentially lose electrons (undergo oxidation). The released electrons flow to the iron, thus preventing iron from losing electrons (i.e., preventing iron from being oxidised or rusting).

Detailed Explanation: A highly frequent DSE explanation question! The keywords are "more reactive metal" and "preferentially loses electrons". As long as iron continuously receives electrons, it cannot turn into $Fe^{2+}$ ions, meaning the rusting process cannot occur. This also explains why galvanised iron remains rust-proof even if the zinc coating is scratched.
