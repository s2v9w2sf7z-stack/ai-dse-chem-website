DSE 化學筆記：課題十一 碳化合物的化學

1. 立體異構 (Stereoisomerism)

立體異構體具有相同的分子式及結構式，但原子的空間排列不同。
  • 順反異構 (Cis-trans)：因 $C=C$ 雙鍵不能自由旋轉而產生。條件是每個雙鍵碳原子上必須連有兩個不同的原子或基團。
  • 對映異構 (Enantiomerism)：分子具有手性碳 (Chiral carbon)（連有4個互不相同的基團），形成一對不能重疊的鏡像。這對異構體的化學和物理性質幾乎完全相同，唯一差別是對偏振光 (plane-polarized light) 的旋轉方向相反。

3. 關鍵化學測試 (Chemical Tests)

DSE 必考：如何區分醛 (Aldehyde) 和酮 (Ketone)？
兩者均含有羰基 ($-C=O$)，因此都會與 2,4-二硝基苯肼 (2,4-DNP) 反應產生黃色/橙色沉澱。
但只有醛具有還原性。加入 多侖試劑 (Tollens' reagent) 並溫水浴加熱，醛會產生銀鏡 (Silver mirror)，而酮則無反應。

DSE Chemistry Notes: Topic 11 Chemistry of Carbon Compounds

1. Stereoisomerism

Stereoisomers have the same molecular and structural formula but different spatial arrangements of atoms.
  • Cis-trans isomerism: Arises from the restricted rotation of a $C=C$ double bond. The condition is that each carbon of the double bond must be attached to two different atoms or groups.
  • Enantiomerism: Occurs when a molecule has a chiral carbon (bonded to 4 completely different groups), forming non-superimposable mirror images. They have identical physical/chemical properties except they rotate plane-polarized light in opposite directions.

3. Key Chemical Tests

DSE Must-Know: How to distinguish Aldehydes and Ketones?
Both contain the carbonyl group ($-C=O$) and will react with 2,4-DNP to give a yellow/orange precipitate.
However, only aldehydes have reducing properties. Add Tollens' reagent and warm in a water bath; aldehyde forms a silver mirror, while ketone gives no reaction.

Q1. 丙-1-醇 ($CH_3CH_2CH_2OH$) 是一種一級醇（Primary alcohol）。當它與酸化重鉻酸鉀溶液 ($K_2Cr_2O_7 / H^+$) 混合並進行加熱回流 (Reflux)時，會生成什麼主要的有機產物？寫出該產物的結構式，並描述溶液預期的顏色變化。

Q1. Propan-1-ol ($CH_3CH_2CH_2OH$) is a primary alcohol. When it is mixed with acidified potassium dichromate solution ($K_2Cr_2O_7 / H^+$) and heated under reflux, what is the major organic product formed? Draw the structural formula of the product and describe the expected colour change of the solution.

查看答案及詳細解析 View Answer and Detailed Explanation

正確答案：
主要有機產物：丙酸 (Propanoic acid)。
結構式：$CH_3CH_2COOH$ （需展示官能基 $-COOH$）
顏色變化：溶液由橙色變為綠色。

精修解析： 【DSE 實驗條件陷阱！】一級醇的氧化分為兩步：先變成「醛（Aldehyde）」，再變成「羧酸（Carboxylic acid）」。本題明確指出實驗條件是**加熱回流（Reflux）**，這意味著蒸氣會不斷冷凝回流繼續反應，迫使一級醇「完全氧化」成羧酸（丙酸）。如果題目改成「蒸餾（Distillation）」，產物則會是丙醛（Propanal）。另外，重鉻酸根離子 ($Cr_2O_7^{2-}$, 橙色) 被還原成鉻(III)離子 ($Cr^{3+}$, 綠色)，這個顏色變化是必背的！

Correct Answer:
Major organic product: Propanoic acid.
Structural formula: $CH_3CH_2COOH$ (showing the $-COOH$ group)
Colour change: The solution changes from orange to green.

Detailed Explanation: [DSE Experimental Condition Trap!] The oxidation of a primary alcohol occurs in two steps: first to an "aldehyde", then to a "carboxylic acid". This question specifies the condition as **heating under reflux**, which means the vapours continuously condense and return to the flask for further reaction, forcing the primary alcohol to be "completely oxidised" into a carboxylic acid (propanoic acid). If the condition were changed to "distillation", the product would be propanal. Additionally, dichromate ions ($Cr_2O_7^{2-}$, orange) are reduced to chromium(III) ions ($Cr^{3+}$, green)—this colour change is a must-memorize!

Q2. 乙酸 ($CH_3COOH$) 與乙醇 ($CH_3CH_2OH$) 在濃硫酸 ($conc. \, H_2SO_4$) 催化並加熱下會發生酯化反應（Esterification）。寫出生成的酯的 IUPAC 名稱。此外，濃硫酸在該反應中擔當了哪兩個重要角色？

Q2. Ethanoic acid ($CH_3COOH$) reacts with ethanol ($CH_3CH_2OH$) under heating with concentrated sulphuric acid ($conc. \, H_2SO_4$) as a catalyst to undergo esterification. State the IUPAC name of the ester formed. Furthermore, what TWO important roles does concentrated sulphuric acid play in this reaction?

查看答案及詳細解析 View Answer and Detailed Explanation

正確答案：
酯的 IUPAC 名稱：乙酸乙酯 (Ethyl ethanoate)。
濃硫酸的角色：1. 催化劑 (Catalyst)（加速反應）；2. 脫水劑 (Dehydrating agent)（吸收反應生成的水，使平衡向右移動，從而提高酯的產率）。

精修解析： 命名酯時，來自「醇」的部分字尾變「酯」（Ethanol $\rightarrow$ Ethyl 乙酯），來自「酸」的部分字首為「某酸」（Ethanoic acid $\rightarrow$ Ethanoate 乙酸），合併為乙酸乙酯。對於濃硫酸角色的提問在 DSE 非常經典，它既能提供 $H^+$ 催化反應，其強烈的脫水性還能移除水分子，根據勒沙特列原理，這會迫使可逆的酯化反應平衡向生成物方向移動，極具含金量！

Correct Answer:
IUPAC name of the ester: Ethyl ethanoate.
Roles of conc. $H_2SO_4$: 1. As a catalyst (to speed up the reaction); 2. As a dehydrating agent (to remove the water formed, shifting the equilibrium to the right and thus increasing the yield of the ester).

Detailed Explanation: When naming an ester, the alkyl part derived from the "alcohol" comes first (ethanol $\rightarrow$ ethyl), followed by the carboxylate part from the "carboxylic acid" (ethanoic acid $\rightarrow$ ethanoate), yielding ethyl ethanoate. The question about the roles of concentrated sulphuric acid is a DSE classic: it not only provides $H^+$ to catalyze the reaction, but its strong dehydrating nature also removes water molecules. According to Le Chatelier's Principle, this forces the equilibrium of this reversible reaction to shift forward, making it a highly valued multi-mark concept!

Q3. 丙酮 ($CH_3COCH_3$) 和丙醇 ($CH_3CH_2CH_2OH$) 是結構不同的同分異構物（Isomers）。描述一個簡單的化學測試（寫出試劑及觀察結果）以辨別這兩種無色液體。

Q3. Propanone ($CH_3COCH_3$) and propan-1-ol ($CH_3CH_2CH_2OH$) are isomers with different structures. Describe a simple chemical test (state the reagent and observations) to distinguish between these two colourless liquids.

查看答案及詳細解析 View Answer and Detailed Explanation

正確答案：（可選擇以下其中一種方案）
方案 A（利用氧化反應）：
測試：加入酸化高錳酸鉀溶液 ($KMnO_4 / H^+$) 並加熱。
現象：丙醇能令紫色溶液褪色（或變無色）；而丙酮則沒有明顯變化（溶液保持紫色）。
方案 B（利用與活潑金屬反應）：
測試：加入一小塊鈉金屬 ($Na$)。
現象：丙醇中會產生無色氣泡（釋放 $H_2$）；而丙酮中則沒有氣泡產生。

精修解析： 辨別碳化合物的關鍵在於對比它們的官能基。丙酮是「酮（Ketone）」，含有羰基 ($C=O$)，極難被進一步氧化，也不與鈉反應；而丙醇是「醇（Alcohol）」，含有羥基 ($-OH$)，既可以被強氧化劑氧化，也能與鈉金屬反應釋出氫氣。在答辨別題時，必須採取「**一有一無**」或「**一變一不變**」的對比描述方式，才能穩拿滿分！

Correct Answer: (Choose one of the following methods)
Method A (Using oxidation):
Test: Add acidified potassium permanganate solution ($KMnO_4 / H^+$) and heat.
Observations: Propan-1-ol will decolorize the purple solution (or turn it colourless), while propanone shows no observable change (the solution remains purple).
Method B (Using reaction with active metal):
Test: Add a small piece of sodium metal ($Na$).
Observations: Colourless gas bubbles are evolved in propan-1-ol, while no bubbles are formed in propanone.

Detailed Explanation: The key to distinguishing carbon compounds lies in comparing their functional groups. Propanone is a "ketone" containing a carbonyl group ($C=O$), which is resistant to further oxidation and does not react with sodium. Propan-1-ol is an "alcohol" containing a hydroxyl group ($-OH$), which can be oxidised by strong oxidising agents and reacts with sodium to release hydrogen gas. When answering distinguishing questions, you must provide a comparative description ("one shows a change while the other does not") to ensure full marks!
