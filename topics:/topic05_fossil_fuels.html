DSE 化學筆記：課題五 化石燃料和碳化合物

1. 石油分餾與物理性質

石油的分餾 (Fractional distillation) 是物理變化。隨著碳原子數目（分子大小）增加，分子間的范德華力增強，導致：

  • 沸點 (Boiling point) 上升。
  • 黏度 (Viscosity) 增加（流動性降低）。
  • 易燃性 (Flammability) 降低，且燃燒時產生更多黑煙。

2. 烷烴與烯烴 (Alkanes vs Alkenes)

核心區別：
烷烴是飽和的，只含 $C-C$ 單鍵；烯烴是不飽和的，含有最少一個 $C=C$ 雙鍵。雙鍵是烯烴的官能基 (Functional group)。

核心化學反應對比：

3. 塑膠與聚合作用 (Plastics & Polymerisation)

透過加成聚合作用，烯烴分子（單體）中的 $C=C$ 雙鍵斷裂，互相連結形成只含 $C-C$ 單鍵的長鏈聚合物。例如，乙烯 ($n \, C_2H_4$) 聚合成聚乙烯 ($-[-CH_2-CH_2-]-_n$)。

DSE Chemistry Notes: Topic 5 Fossil Fuels and Carbon Compounds

1. Fractional Distillation of Petroleum

Fractional distillation of petroleum is a physical process. As the number of carbon atoms (molecular size) increases, the Van der Waals' forces between molecules become stronger, resulting in:

  • Higher boiling points.
  • Higher viscosity (less flowable).
  • Lower flammability and more sooty flame upon combustion.

2. Alkanes vs Alkenes

Core Differences:
Alkanes are saturated and contain only $C-C$ single bonds. Alkenes are unsaturated and contain at least one $C=C$ double bond, which acts as their functional group.

Comparison of Key Reactions:

3. Plastics & Polymerisation

Through addition polymerisation, the $C=C$ double bonds in alkene molecules (monomers) break and join together to form a long-chain polymer containing only $C-C$ single bonds. For example, ethene ($n \, C_2H_4$) polymerizes into polyethene ($-[-CH_2-CH_2-]-_n$).

Q1. 實驗室有兩樽失去標籤的無色液體，分別是己烷 (Hexane) 和己烯 (Hexene)。描述一個化學測試來辨別這兩種液體，並寫出預期的實驗觀察結果。

Q1. There are two unlabelled bottles of colourless liquids in the laboratory, which are hexane and hexene. Describe a chemical test to distinguish between these two liquids, and state the expected experimental observations.

查看答案及詳細解析 View Answer and Detailed Explanation

正確答案：
測試方法：將溴溶於有機溶劑（或加入溴水），並將混合物置於黑暗環境 (in the dark) 中。
觀察結果：己烯會令橙色的溴溶液迅速褪色 (decolorizes rapidly)，而己烷則沒有明顯顏色變化（溶液保持橙色）。

精修解析： 這是 DSE 有機化學中最經典的測試題！答題時必須寫出「在黑暗中」這個條件，因為如果在紫外光照射下，己烷也會與溴發生「取代反應」而褪色。己烯發生的是「加成反應」，不需要光照也能迅速進行。

Correct Answer:
Test: Add bromine dissolved in an organic solvent (or bromine water) to the samples and place the mixtures in the dark.
Observations: Hexene will rapidly decolorize the orange bromine solution, while hexane shows no observable colour change (the solution remains orange).

Detailed Explanation: This is the most classic chemical test question in DSE organic chemistry! You MUST mention the condition "in the dark", because under UV light, hexane would also undergo a "substitution reaction" with bromine and decolorize it. Hexene undergoes an "addition reaction", which occurs rapidly even without light.

Q2. 在石油的分餾 (Fractional distillation) 過程中，柴油餾分 (Diesel fraction) 的沸點比汽油餾分 (Petrol fraction) 高。從分子層面解釋這個現象。

Q2. In the fractional distillation of petroleum, the diesel fraction has a higher boiling point than the petrol fraction. Explain this phenomenon at the molecular level.

查看答案及詳細解析 View Answer and Detailed Explanation

正確答案： 柴油餾分含有體積較大（碳原子數目較多/分子質量較大）的碳氫化合物分子。分子越大，分子間的范德華力 (Van der Waals' forces) 越強。因此，需要更多的熱能來克服這些分子間引力，導致其沸點較高。

精修解析： 解釋沸點差異的標準 2 分題。答題框架分為兩步：第一步比較分子大小/電子數量；第二步明確指出「范德華力」的強弱差異。切記，分離石油是物理變化，克服的絕對是「分子間的范德華力」，千萬不要寫成克服共價鍵！

Correct Answer: The diesel fraction contains larger hydrocarbon molecules (with more carbon atoms / higher molecular mass). The larger the molecules, the stronger the Van der Waals' forces between them. Therefore, more heat energy is required to overcome these intermolecular forces, resulting in a higher boiling point.

Detailed Explanation: A standard 2-mark question for explaining boiling point differences. The answering framework has two steps: first, compare molecular size/number of electrons; second, explicitly state the strength of the "Van der Waals' forces". Remember, separating petroleum is a physical change involving the overcoming of intermolecular Van der Waals' forces, NEVER write "breaking covalent bonds"!

Q3. 寫出由乙烯 ($C_2H_4$) 轉化為聚乙烯 (Polyethene, PE) 的聚合作用類型，並寫出聚乙烯的重複單位 (Repeating unit)。

Q3. Name the type of polymerisation for the conversion of ethene ($C_2H_4$) to polyethene (PE), and write the repeating unit of polyethene.

正確答案：
聚合作用類型：加成聚合作用 (Addition polymerisation)。

重複單位 (Repeating unit)：

Correct Answer:
Type of polymerisation: Addition polymerisation.

Repeating unit:

-CH₂-CH₂-

精修解析： 加成聚合作用是指大量不飽和單體（含有 $C=C$ 雙鍵）互相連接而沒有副產物生成的反應。在畫或寫「重複單位」時，切記**兩端必須要有延伸出去的鍵（一橫）**，這代表聚合物長鏈可以繼續連接下去。如果沒有畫兩端的橫線，會被扣分！

Detailed Explanation: Addition polymerisation is a reaction where numerous unsaturated monomers (containing $C=C$ double bonds) join together without the formation of any by-products. When drawing or writing a "repeating unit", it is crucial that **there must be extension bonds (dashes) at both ends**, representing that the long polymer chain continues. Marks will be deducted if these end bonds are missing!
