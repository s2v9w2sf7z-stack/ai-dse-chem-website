DSE 化學筆記：課題十 化學平衡

1. 動態平衡的兩大特徵

在一個密閉系統中，當達到動態平衡時：
1. 正向反應速率 = 逆向反應速率 (Rate of forward reaction = Rate of backward reaction)。
2. 系統中所有反應物和生成物的濃度保持不變。

2. 平衡常數 ($K_c$)

對於反應： $aA(aq) + bB(aq) \rightleftharpoons cC(aq) + dD(aq)$

$$K_c = \frac{[C]^c[D]^d}{[A]^a[B]^b}$$
DSE 必考陷阱：
1. 寫 $K_c$ 表達式時，純固體 $(s)$ 和純液體 $(l)$（如作為溶劑的水）的濃度不包括在內。
2. 只有溫度的改變會改變 $K_c$ 的數值。濃度、壓強或加入催化劑都不會改變 $K_c$。

3. 勒沙得利爾原理 (Le Chatelier's Principle)

當系統受到改變時，平衡位置會移動以抵消該改變 (oppose the change)。

干擾因素系統反應 (平衡移動方向)
增加反應物濃度向右移動（以消耗反應物）。
增加壓強（減小體積）向氣體分子總數較少的一方移動。
升高溫度向吸熱反應 (Endothermic, $\Delta H > 0$) 的方向移動。
加入催化劑不移動。（只會令系統更快達到平衡）

4. 哈柏法 (Haber Process) 製氨

反應： $N_2(g) + 3H_2(g) \rightleftharpoons 2NH_3(g) \quad \Delta H = -92 \, kJ \, mol^{-1}$

由於這是一個正向為放熱、且正向氣體分子數目減少的反應。理論上低溫高壓能獲得最高產量。但為了平衡反應速率及生產成本，工業上採用：400-500°C、200 大氣壓、鐵催化劑的折衷條件。

DSE Chemistry Notes: Topic 10 Chemical Equilibrium

1. Two Characteristics of Dynamic Equilibrium

In a closed system, when dynamic equilibrium is reached:
1. Rate of forward reaction = Rate of backward reaction.
2. The concentrations of all reactants and products remain constant.

2. Equilibrium Constant ($K_c$)

For the reaction: $aA(aq) + bB(aq) \rightleftharpoons cC(aq) + dD(aq)$

$$K_c = \frac{[C]^c[D]^d}{[A]^a[B]^b}$$
DSE Traps:
1. Concentrations of pure solids $(s)$ and pure liquids $(l)$ (e.g., water as a solvent) are not included in the $K_c$ expression.
2. ONLY Temperature can change the value of $K_c$. Changes in concentration, pressure, or adding a catalyst do NOT change $K_c$.

3. Le Chatelier's Principle

When a system at equilibrium is subjected to a change, the equilibrium position shifts to oppose the change.

DisturbanceSystem's Response (Direction of Shift)
Increase concentration of reactantShifts to the right (to consume the reactant).
Increase pressure (Decrease volume)Shifts to the side with fewer moles of gas molecules.
Increase temperatureShifts to the endothermic direction ($\Delta H > 0$).
Add a catalystNo shift. (Only helps reach equilibrium faster).

4. Haber Process (Production of Ammonia)

Reaction: $N_2(g) + 3H_2(g) \rightleftharpoons 2NH_3(g) \quad \Delta H = -92 \, kJ \, mol^{-1}$

Since the forward reaction is exothermic and proceeds with a decrease in gas molecules, low temperature and high pressure theoretically give the maximum yield. However, considering reaction rate and cost, industry uses compromise conditions: 400-500°C, 200 atm, and an Iron catalyst.

✍️ 自我檢測練習（課題十：化學平衡） ✍️ Self-Assessment Quiz (Topic 10: Chemical Equilibrium)

Q1. 在一個密閉容器中達到以下平衡：$N_2O_4(g) \rightleftharpoons 2NO_2(g)$。已知 $N_2O_4$ 為無色氣體，$NO_2$ 為啡色氣體。若在恆溫下，突然將容器的體積減半（即壓強增大），描述混合氣體的「即時（Instantaneous）」及「最終（Long-term）」顏色變化，並利用勒沙特列原理簡述原因。

Q1. The following equilibrium is attained in a sealed container: $N_2O_4(g) \rightleftharpoons 2NO_2(g)$. $N_2O_4$ is a colourless gas and $NO_2$ is a brown gas. If the volume of the container is suddenly halved (i.e., pressure is increased) at constant temperature, describe the "instantaneous" and "long-term" colour changes of the gas mixture, and briefly explain using Le Chatelier's Principle.

查看答案及詳細解析 View Answer and Detailed Explanation

正確答案：
即時顏色變化：啡色立即變深。
最終顏色變化：啡色稍微變淡，但仍比未壓縮前深色。
原因解析：
1. 當體積減半，所有氣體的濃度（包括 $NO_2$）在即時會加倍，因此顏色立即變深。
2. 根據勒沙特列原理，系統會向減少氣體總分子數（即減少壓強）的方向移動。由於左邊只有 1 摩爾氣體而右邊有 2 摩爾，平衡會向左移動，消耗 $NO_2$ 並生成 $N_2O_4$，因此啡色隨後會稍微變淡。

精修解析： 【DSE 兩大黃金死穴！】第一，很多同學一看到壓強增加就直接答「平衡向左移，所以顏色變淡」，完全忽略了「體積變小導致即時濃度上升」的即時階段，直接痛失半分！第二，緊記平衡移動「只能減弱但無法完全消除」外來的改變，所以最終的啡色雖然比即時淡，但依然比最初未壓縮前要**深色**！

Correct Answer:
Instantaneous colour change: The brown colour deepens immediately.
Long-term colour change: The brown colour fades slightly, but remains darker than the original state.
Explanation:
1. When the volume is halved, the concentration of all gases (including $NO_2$) doubles instantaneously, hence the color deepens immediately.
2. According to Le Chatelier's Principle, the system will shift in a direction that decreases the total number of gas molecules (to reduce the pressure). Since there is 1 mole of gas on the left and 2 moles on the right, the equilibrium will shift to the left, consuming $NO_2$ and forming $N_2O_4$. Thus, the brown colour subsequently fades slightly.

Detailed Explanation: [Two Deadly DSE Traps!] First, many students see an increase in pressure and immediately jump to "equilibrium shifts left, so the color fades", completely missing the instantaneous stage where the concentration spikes due to volume reduction—losing half the marks instantly! Second, remember that equilibrium shift can "only contrast but never completely override" the external change, so the final brown colour is still **darker** than it originally was before compression!

Q2. 寫出哈柏法製氨反應：$N_2(g) + 3H_2(g) \rightleftharpoons 2NH_3(g)$ 的平衡常數（$K_c$）表達式，並指出該 $K_c$ 的單位。若在該系統中加入催化劑， $K_c$ 的數值會否改變？試加以解釋。

Q2. Write the equilibrium constant ($K_c$) expression for the Haber Process: $N_2(g) + 3H_2(g) \rightleftharpoons 2NH_3(g)$, and state its units. If a catalyst is added to the system, will the value of $K_c$ change? Explain your answer.

查看答案及詳細解析 View Answer and Detailed Explanation

正確答案： Correct Answer:

K_c = \frac{[NH_3]^2}{[N_2][H_2]^3}

單位：$mol^{-2} \, dm^6$ (或 $mol^{-2} \cdot dm^6$ / $\frac{1}{mol^2 \, dm^{-6}}$)
數值變化：不會改變。
原因： 催化劑能以同等程度加速正向和逆向反應，使系統更快達到平衡，但不會改變平衡時各物質的濃度比例。在 DSE 課程中，平衡常數 $K_c$ 的數值只受溫度影響 (Only affected by temperature)。

精修解析： 寫 $K_c$ 表達式時必須用方括號 `[ ]` 代表平衡濃度。求單位的步驟是把 $mol \, dm^{-3}$ 代入表達式中進行消元：$\frac{(mol \, dm^{-3})^2}{(mol \, dm^{-3}) \cdot (mol \, dm^{-3})^3} = \frac{1}{(mol \, dm^{-3})^2} = mol^{-2} \, dm^6$。另外，「$K_c$ 只受溫度影響」是 DSE MC 題的「終極提分密碼」，不論題目說改變壓強、改變濃度還是加催化劑，只要溫度不變，$K_c$ 就絕對不變！

Units: $mol^{-2} \, dm^6$
Change in value: Will not change.
Reason: A catalyst increases the rates of both forward and reverse reactions to the same extent, allowing the system to reach equilibrium faster, but it does not alter the concentration ratio of substances at equilibrium. In the DSE curriculum, the value of the equilibrium constant $K_c$ is only affected by temperature.

Detailed Explanation: When writing the $K_c$ expression, square brackets `[ ]` must be used to represent equilibrium concentrations. To find the units, substitute $mol \, dm^{-3}$ into the expression and cancel out: $\frac{(mol \, dm^{-3})^2}{(mol \, dm^{-3}) \cdot (mol \, dm^{-3})^3} = \frac{1}{(mol \, dm^{-3})^2} = mol^{-2} \, dm^6$. Furthermore, "K_c is only affected by temperature" is the ultimate cheat code for DSE MC questions; no matter if the question alters pressure, concentration, or adds a catalyst, as long as the temperature is constant, $K_c$ remains absolutely unchanged!

Q3. 對於某個可逆反應：$2A(g) \rightleftharpoons B(g) + C(g)$。實驗數據顯示，當溫度由 298 K 升高至 400 K 時，其平衡常數 $K_c$ 的數值顯著減少。試推導並解釋該反應的正向反應（Forward reaction）是放熱（Exothermic）還是吸熱（Endothermic）反應。

Q3. For a reversible reaction: $2A(g) \rightleftharpoons B(g) + C(g)$. Experimental data shows that when the temperature is increased from 298 K to 400 K, the value of its equilibrium constant $K_c$ decreases significantly. Deduce and explain whether the forward reaction is exothermic or endothermic.

查看答案及詳細解析 View Answer and Detailed Explanation

正確答案： 正向反應是放熱反應 (Exothermic reaction)。

推導及解釋：
1. 當溫度升高時，$K_c$ 的數值減少，這意味著平衡系統中生成物（$B$ 和 $C$）的濃度減少，而反應物（$A$）的濃度增加，顯示平衡向左（逆向）移動。
2. 根據勒沙特列原理，升高溫度時，系統會向吸熱（Endothermic）的方向移動以吸收外加的熱能。
3. 既然升溫導致平衡向左移動，說明逆向反應是吸熱反應；因此，其相反方向的**正向反應必然是放熱反應**。

精修解析： 【DSE 滿分思維鏈】這是一道非常漂亮的邏輯推導題。答題時一定要分三步走：第一步由「$K_c$ 變小」推導出「平衡向左移」；第二步由「升溫」聯想到勒沙特列原理中的「向吸熱方向移」；第三步結合兩者得出「逆向為吸熱，正向為放熱」。這種結構清晰的回答，在 DSE 卷一長題目中能穩拿滿分 3 分。

Correct Answer: The forward reaction is an exothermic reaction.

Deduction & Explanation:
1. When the temperature increases, the value of $K_c$ decreases. This means the concentrations of products ($B$ and $C$) decrease while the concentration of reactant ($A$) increases at equilibrium, indicating that the equilibrium has shifted to the left (reverse direction).
2. According to Le Chatelier's Principle, when temperature is increased, the system shifts in the endothermic direction to absorb the added heat energy.
3. Since increasing the temperature shifts the equilibrium to the left, the reverse reaction must be endothermic. Therefore, the opposite direction—the **forward reaction—must be exothermic**.

Detailed Explanation: [DSE Full-mark Thinking Chain] This is a brilliant logical deduction question. Your answer must follow a 3-step sequence: Step 1, deduce "equilibrium shifts left" from "decrease in $K_c$"; Step 2, link "temperature increase" to Le Chatelier's "shifts in endothermic direction"; Step 3, combine both to conclude "reverse is endothermic, so forward is exothermic". This clear-cut structure guarantees full 3 marks in DSE Paper 1 structured questions.
