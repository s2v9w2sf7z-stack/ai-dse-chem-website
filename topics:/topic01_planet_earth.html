DSE 化學筆記：課題一 地球

核心概念摘要

  • 大氣：空氣是混合物。工業上透過液態空氣的分餾提取氮氣和氧氣，依賴沸點差異分離。
  • 海洋：海水含有大量溶解鹽。
    • 測試鈉離子 ($Na^+$)：進行焰色試驗，呈現金黃色火焰。
    • 測試氯離子 ($Cl^-$)：加入稀硝酸及硝酸銀溶液，產生白色沉澱物。
      $Ag^+(aq) + Cl^-(aq) \rightarrow AgCl(s)$
  • 岩石與礦物：石灰石主要成分為碳酸鈣 ($CaCO_3$)。
    • 受熱分解：
      $CaCO_3(s) \rightarrow CaO(s) + CO_2(g)$
    • 與稀酸反應：
      $CaCO_3(s) + 2HCl(aq) \rightarrow CaCl_2(aq) + H_2O(l) + CO_2(g)$

DSE Chemistry Notes: Topic 1 Planet Earth

Core Concepts Summary

  • The Atmosphere: Air is a mixture. Nitrogen and oxygen are extracted industrially by the fractional distillation of liquid air, based on differences in boiling points.
  • The Ocean: Sea water contains abundant dissolved salts.
    • Test for Sodium ion ($Na^+$): Perform a flame test, showing a golden yellow flame.
    • Test for Chloride ion ($Cl^-$): Add dilute nitric acid and silver nitrate solution, forming a white precipitate.
      $Ag^+(aq) + Cl^-(aq) \rightarrow AgCl(s)$
  • Rocks and Minerals: The main component of limestone is calcium carbonate ($CaCO_3$).
    • Thermal decomposition:
      $CaCO_3(s) \rightarrow CaO(s) + CO_2(g)$
    • Reaction with dilute acids:
      $CaCO_3(s) + 2HCl(aq) \rightarrow CaCl_2(aq) + H_2O(l) + CO_2(g)$
