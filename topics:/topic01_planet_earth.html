DSE 化學筆記：課題一 地球

核心概念摘要

  • 大氣：空氣是混合物。工業上透過液態空氣的分餾提取氮氣和氧氣，依賴沸點差異分離。
  • 海洋：海水含有大量溶解鹽。
    • 測試鈉離子 ($Na^+$)：進行焰色試驗，呈現金黃色火焰。
    • 測試氯離子 ($Cl^-$)：加入稀硝酸及硝酸銀溶液，產生白色沉澱物。
      $Ag^+(aq) + Cl^-(aq) \rightarrow AgCl(s)$
  • 岩石與礦物：石灰石主要成分為碳酸鈣 ($CaCO_3$)。
    • 受熱分解：
      $CaCO_3(s) \rightarrow CaO(s) + CO_2(g)$
    • 與稀酸反應：
      $CaCO_3(s) + 2HCl(aq) \rightarrow CaCl_2(aq) + H_2O(l) + CO_2(g)$

DSE Chemistry Notes: Topic 1 Planet Earth

Core Concepts Summary

  • The Atmosphere: Air is a mixture. Nitrogen and oxygen are extracted industrially by the fractional distillation of liquid air, based on differences in boiling points.
  • The Ocean: Sea water contains abundant dissolved salts.
    • Test for Sodium ion ($Na^+$): Perform a flame test, showing a golden yellow flame.
    • Test for Chloride ion ($Cl^-$): Add dilute nitric acid and silver nitrate solution, forming a white precipitate.
      $Ag^+(aq) + Cl^-(aq) \rightarrow AgCl(s)$
  • Rocks and Minerals: The main component of limestone is calcium carbonate ($CaCO_3$).
    • Thermal decomposition:
      $CaCO_3(s) \rightarrow CaO(s) + CO_2(g)$
    • Reaction with dilute acids:
      $CaCO_3(s) + 2HCl(aq) \rightarrow CaCl_2(aq) + H_2O(l) + CO_2(g)$

✍️ 自我檢測練習（課題一：地球） ✍️ Self-Assessment Quiz (Topic 1: Planet Earth)

Q1. 在工業上，從液態空氣中分離氧氣和氮氣常用什麼方法？這個方法是建立在兩種氣體的哪項物理性質差異上？

Q1. What industrial method is commonly used to separate oxygen and nitrogen from liquid air? On which difference in physical property of the two gases is this method based?

查看答案及詳細解析 View Answer and Detailed Explanation

正確答案： 分餾法 (Fractional distillation)；建立在兩種氣體的沸點 (Boiling points)差異上。

精修解析： 空氣先被液化，然後逐步加熱。由於氮的沸點（-196°C）比氧的沸點（-183°C）低，氮氣會首先蒸發並在分餾柱頂部被收集，從而達到分離效果。這是 DSE 關於大氣部分必考的物理分離法。

Correct Answer: Fractional distillation; based on the difference in their boiling points.

Detailed Explanation: Air is first liquefied and then warmed up gradually. Since nitrogen has a lower boiling point (-196°C) than oxygen (-183°C), nitrogen gas boils off first and is collected at the top of the fractionating column. This is a must-know physical separation method in DSE.

Q2. 描述如何進行化學測試以證實海水中存在氯離子 ($Cl^-$)，並寫出預期的實驗觀察結果。

Q2. Describe a chemical test to confirm the presence of chloride ions ($Cl^-$) in seawater, and state the expected observation.

查看答案及詳細解析 View Answer and Detailed Explanation

正確答案： 向海水中加入過量的稀硝酸 (Dilute nitric acid)，然後加入硝酸銀溶液 (Silver nitrate solution)。預期觀察到產生白色沉澱 (White precipitate)。

精修解析： 銀離子與氯離子反應會生成不溶於水的氯化銀白色沉澱。加入稀硝酸的作用是為了消除可能存在的碳酸根離子 ($CO_3^{2-}$) 的干擾，因為碳酸銀也是白色沉澱。離子方程式為：$Ag^+(aq) + Cl^-(aq) \rightarrow AgCl(s)$。

Correct Answer: Add excess dilute nitric acid to the seawater sample, followed by silver nitrate solution. Expected observation: A white precipitate is formed.

Detailed Explanation: Silver ions react with chloride ions to form a white precipitate of silver chloride which is insoluble in water. Dilute nitric acid is added to eliminate interference from carbonate ions ($CO_3^{2-}$), as silver carbonate is also a white precipitate. The ionic equation is: $Ag^+(aq) + Cl^-(aq) \rightarrow AgCl(s)$.

Q3. 寫出石灰石（主要成分為碳酸鈣 $CaCO_3$）在強熱下發生熱分解（Thermal decomposition）的化學方程式，並註明物態符號。

Q3. Write the chemical equation for the thermal decomposition of limestone (mainly calcium carbonate $CaCO_3$) under strong heating, including state symbols.

查看答案及詳細解析 View Answer and Detailed Explanation

正確答案： Correct Answer:

CaCO₃ (s) → CaO (s) + CO₂ (g)

精修解析： 碳酸鈣（固態）受熱會分解成氧化鈣（生石灰，固態）和二氧化碳（氣態）。在考試中，物態符號 (s) 和 (g) 是極易被忽略的扣分點，務必小心！另外，產生的 $CO_2$ 氣體可用石灰水進行檢驗（石灰水變渾濁）。

Detailed Explanation: Calcium carbonate (solid) decomposes upon heating to form calcium oxide (quicklime, solid) and carbon dioxide (gas). State symbols (s) and (g) are frequently penalized if omitted in exams, so make sure to include them! The $CO_2$ gas produced can be tested using limewater (turns milky).
